Stage Dive
100 Mana Cooldown: 75 seconds
Leap to target location, landing after 2.75 seconds, dealing 330 damage to enemies in the area, and Slowing them by 50% for 3 seconds.
    Type: Damage / Mobility: Teleport
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Channeled cast, Stasis, Slow
    Cast time: 1.25+(1.5)+0 seconds
    Range: Global
    Radius: 5
  • Interrupting Stage Dive during its windup puts it on a 10-second cooldown.
  • Stace Dive cannot be interrupted or prevented by Roots, unlike most other mobility based Abilities.
  • After channel, E.T.C is put in Stasis until landing, making him immune to damage and removing Cleansable debuffs and Armor debuffs.
  • Warning AoE appears when the Ability has been cast successfully.
