Skills
Image Transmission
*Cooldown 25 seconds*
Activate to switch places with a target Mirror Image, removing most negative effects from Samuro and the Mirror Image.
Type: Movement: Teleport
Affects: Self, Images
Targeting: Unit target
Properties: Stasis
Cast time: 0+0.125 seconds
Range: Global
  • Samuro can not use Image Transmission on target Image that is Stunned, Silenced, in Stasis or in Time Stop.
  • Using Image Transission to swap between Images puts Samuro and his Images in a special Stasis for 0.125 seconds, when the Ability is being cast, removing debuffs from them and making them briefly untargetable and immune to damage, regardless of which Image was targetted.
  • All of Samuro's Mirror Images' Health is set to match the percentage of Samuro's current Health, when the swap is finished.
  • Using Image Transmission during Wind Walk's Unrevealable period doesn't break Stealth, but using it after Unrevealable period is over will. Real Samuro retains Stealth over the swap.
Advancing Strikes
Basic Attacks against enemy Heroes increase Samuro's Movement Speed by 25% for 2 seconds.
Properties: Attack Modifier
  • Advancing Strikes and the Talents enhancing it work independently on Samuro and each of his Mirror Images.

Mirror Image
*Cooldown 18 seconds*
Teleport a short distance in the direction of the mouse cursor, creating 2 images for 18 seconds with 100% of Samuro's current Health that Basic Attack enemies for 9 damage. Whenever an Image takes damage, it deals that amount of damage to itself, effectively doubling the damage it takes.Maximum 2 Mirror images can be active at a time. Using Mirror Image removes most negative effects from Samuro.
Type: Summon / Movement: Teleport
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Clamping, Stasis
Cast time: 0+0.125 seconds
Range: 3.5
Mirror Image
Type: Heroic Pet, Clone
Scaling: 4.00% (damage)
Targeting: Automatic
Health: 50%
Basic Attack damage: 9
Attack speed: 100%
Attack range: 100%
Movement Speed: 100%
Sight radius: 100%
Leash radius: -
  • Samuro appears in the direction of the player's Mouse Cursor, when Mirror Image is cast. Samuro and his Mirror Images spawn in an equilateral triangle around the point where Samuro was standing when the ability was cast.
  • Using Mirror Image puts Samuro in a special Stasis for 0.125 seconds when the Ability is being cast, removing debuffs from Samuro and making him briefly untargetable and immune to damage.
  • Mirror Images deal the same type of damage than Samuro does.
  • Mirror Images gain all benefits from Samuro's Trait and Talents affecting it.
  • Mirror Images can be affected by Regeneration Globes' healing, but not pick them up themselves. The effect is not shared between Samuro and his Images.
  • Killing Mirror Images rewards no XP or Takedown credit.
  • All Mirror Images are destroyed, when real Samuro successfully Hearths.
W
Critical Strike
*Cooldown 10 seconds*
Samuro's next Basic Attack within 8 seconds will be a Critical Strike, dealing 50% increased damage. This also applies to Images, and does not break Wind Walk. ***Passive:*** Samuro and his Images deal a Critical Strike on every 4th Basic Attack.
Type: Damage modifier
Affects: Self, Images
Targeting: No target
Properties: Attack Modifier
Cast time: Instant
  • Does not stack with itself. Activating sets Samuro's next attack to be critical strike (regardless of if it already was), putting the ability on cooldown.
  • Stacks additively with other damage modifiers affecting Samuro's Basic Attack damage.
  • Causes Samuro's next Basic Attack to strike immediately.

Wind Walk
*Cooldown 15 seconds*
Grant Samuro Stealth for up to 10 seconds or until he attacks, uses an Ability, or takes damage.While Stealthed, Samuro's Movement Speed is increased by 25% and he can pass through other units. Remaining stationary for at least 1.5 seconds while Stealthed grants Invisible.Samuro is Unrevealable for the first 1 second of Wind Walk.
Type: Status effect
Affects: Self
Targeting: No target
Properties: Stealth
Cast time: Instant
  • Using Mirror Image and Mounting during Unrevealable period will break Stealth once the period ends.

R
Illusion Master
*Cooldown 10 seconds*
Mirror Images can be controlled individually or as a group and their damage is increased by 100%. ***Passive:*** Image Transmission's cooldown is reduced to 10 seconds.
Type: Damage modifier
Affects: Images
  • Illusion bonus damage stacks additively with other damage modifiers.
  • Illusion Master active shares cooldown with Image Transmission, and has all the same properties.
  • Mirror Images have a copy of Illusion Master active and Image Transmission, both of which swap the Image using it with Samuro. The cooldown is shared between Samuro and all his Images.
  • Mirror Images can Mount, Hearth and drink from Healing Fountains. The effect and cooldowns are independent from Samuro and his other Images, including those that have expired.
  • Images can fake capturing Objectives.
  • A Mirror Image Hearthing does not destroy any Mirror Images, but real Samuro Hearthing still does.
