Skills

Image Transmission
Cooldown: 25 seconds
Activate to switch places with a target Mirror Image, removing most negative effects from Samuro and the Mirror Image.
Type: Movement: Teleport
Affects: Self, Images
Targeting: Unit target
Properties: Stasis
Cast time: 0+0.125 seconds
Range: Global
  • Samuro can not use Image Transmission on target Image that is Stunned, Silenced, in Stasis or in Time Stop.
  • Using Image Transission to swap between Images puts Samuro and his Images in a special Stasis for 0.125 seconds, when the Ability is being cast, removing debuffs from them and making them briefly untargetable and immune to damage, regardless of which Image was targetted.
  • All of Samuro's Mirror Images' Health is set to match the percentage of Samuro's current Health, when the swap is finished.
  • Using Image Transmission during Wind Walk's Unrevealable period doesn't break Stealth, but using it after Unrevealable period is over will. Real Samuro retains Stealth over the swap.

Advancing Strikes
Basic Attacks against enemy Heroes increase Samuro's Movement Speed by 25% for 2 seconds.
Properties: Attack Modifier
  • Advancing Strikes and the Talents enhancing it work independently on Samuro and each of his Mirror Images.
Q
Mirror Image
Cooldown: 18 seconds
Teleport a short distance in the direction of the mouse cursor, creating 2 images for 18 seconds with 100% of Samuro's current Health that Basic Attack enemies for 9 damage. Whenever an Image takes damage, it deals that amount of damage to itself, effectively doubling the damage it takes.
Maximum 2 Mirror images can be active at a time. Using Mirror Image removes most negative effects from Samuro.
Type: Summon / Movement: Teleport
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Clamping, Stasis
Cast time: 0+0.125 seconds
Range: 3.5
Mirror Image
Type: Heroic Pet, Clone
Scaling: 4.00% (damage)
Targeting: Automatic
Health: 50%
Basic Attack damage: 9
Attack speed: 100%
Attack range: 100%
Movement Speed: 100%
Sight radius: 100%
Leash radius: -
  • Samuro appears in the direction of the player's Mouse Cursor, when Mirror Image is cast. Samuro and his Mirror Images spawn in an equilateral triangle around the point where Samuro was standing when the ability was cast.
  • Using Mirror Image puts Samuro in a special Stasis for 0.125 seconds when the Ability is being cast, removing debuffs from Samuro and making him briefly untargetable and immune to damage.
  • Mirror Images deal the same type of damage than Samuro does.
  • Mirror Images gain all benefits from Samuro's Trait and Talents affecting it.
  • Mirror Images can be affected by Regeneration Globes' healing, but not pick them up themselves. The effect is not shared between Samuro and his Images.
  • Killing Mirror Images rewards no XP or Takedown credit.
  • All Mirror Images are destroyed, when real Samuro successfully Hearths.
W
Critical Strike
Cooldown: 10 seconds
Samuro's next Basic Attack within 8 seconds will be a Critical Strike, dealing 50% increased damage. This also applies to Images, and does not break Wind Walk.
Passive: Samuro and his Images deal a Critical Strike on every 4th Basic Attack.
Type: Damage modifier
Affects: Self, Images
Targeting: No target
Properties: Attack Modifier
Cast time: Instant
  • Does not stack with itself. Activating sets Samuro's next attack to be critical strike (regardless of if it already was), putting the ability on cooldown.
  • Stacks additively with other damage modifiers affecting Samuro's Basic Attack damage.
  • Causes Samuro's next Basic Attack to strike immediately.
E
Wind Walk
Cooldown: 15 seconds
GRant Samuro Stealth for up to 10 seconds or until he attacks, uses an Ability, or takes damage.
While Stealthed, Samuro's Movement Speed is increased by 25% and he can pass through other units. Remaining stationary for at least 1.5 seconds while Stealthed grants Invisible.
Samuro is Unrevealable for the first 1 second of Wind Walk.
Type: Status effect
Affects: Self
Targeting: No target
Properties: Stealth
Cast time: Instant
  • Using Mirror Image and Mounting during Unrevealable period will break Stealth once the period ends.
R
Bladestorm
Cooldown: 110 / 0.5 seconds
Cause a Bladestorm of destructive force around Samuro for 4 seconds, making him Unstoppable and dealing 235 damage per second to nearby enemies.
Type: Spell damage / Status effect
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: No target
Properties: Area of Effect, Mobile channel, Unstoppable
Cast time: Instant
Area of Effect: Circle
Radius: 4.25
Tickrate: 5.0 per second (x20)
Damage per tick: 47
  • Bladestorm can be ended prematurely by reactivating the ability.
  • Samuro can move, but not use any abilities, while Bladestorm is active.
R
Illusion Master
Cooldown: 10 seconds
Mirror Images can be controlled individually or as a group and their damage is increased by 100%.
Passive: Image Transmission's cooldown is reduced to 10 seconds.
Type: Damage modifier
Affects: Images
  • Illusion bonus damage stacks additively with other damage modifiers.
  • Illusion Master active shares cooldown with Image Transmission, and has all the same properties.
  • Mirror Images have a copy of Illusion Master active and Image Transmission, both of which swap the Image using it with Samuro. The cooldown is shared between Samuro and all his Images.
  • Mirror Images can Mount, Hearth and drink from Healing Fountains. The effect and cooldowns are independent from Samuro and his other Images, including those that have expired.
  • Images can fake capturing Objectives.
  • A Mirror Image Hearthing does not destroy any Mirror Images, but real Samuro Hearthing still does.

Talents

Way of Illusion
❢ Quest: Every time a Mirror Image Critically Strikes a Hero, Samuro gains 0.25 Attack Damage, up to 10.
❢ Reward: After hitting 40 Heroes, Samuro gains an additional 20 Attack Damage.
Type: Bonus damage
Scaling: -
Affects: Self
Way of the Blade
Critical Strike now happens for Samuro and his Mirror Images every 3rd Basic Attack. Samuro's Critical Strikes against enemy Heroes reduces their Physical Armor by 10 for 2 seconds.
Type: Damage modifier: Physical Armor
Affects: Enemies
Way of the Wind
Gain 40% Movement Speed for 3 seconds after entering or exiting Wind Walk. Samuro's Critical Strikes reduce the cooldown of Windwalk by 1.5 seconds.
4
Mirage
Mirror Image grants Samuro and his Images 40 Spell Armor against the next 2 sources of Spell damage, reducing their damage by 40%.
Type: Damage modifier: Spell Armor
Affects: Self, Images
Properties: Spell Block
One With The Wind
While Samuro has Wind Walk and for 3 seconds after breaking it, both he and his Mirror Images gain 30 Armor, taking 30% reduced damage from all sources.
Type: Damage modifier: Armor
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Self, Images
Deflection
Advancing Strikes grants Samuro and his Mirror Images 30 Physical Armor.
Type: Damage modifier: Physical Armor
Affects: Self, Images
7
Burning Blade
Critical Strikes from Samuro and Mirror Images deal an additional 50% of their Basic Attack damage to the target and nearby enemies.
Type: Spell damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Properties: Attack Modifier, Splash
Radius: 2.5
  • Samuro's Burning Blade attacks deals 55 damage around the target and his Images' attacks 4.5 damage.
  • Samuro's Burning Blade attacks benefit from 50% of flat damage bonuses from Way of Illusion.
  • Burning Blade damage is unaffected by Basic Attack damage modifiers, like Critical Strike or Illusion Master's Image bonus damage, but can be modified by Spell Power.
Phantom Pain
Samuro's Critical Strikes deal an additional 45% of his Basic Attack damage for each Mirror Image that is active.
Type: Damage modifier
Affects: Self
  • Stacks additively with other damage modifiers affecting Samuro's Basic Attack damage.
Crushing Blows
Critical Strike now has 2 charges. Samuro's Basic Attacks against Heroes reduce the cooldown of Critical Strike by 1.75 seconds.
Charge cooldown: 0.5 seconds
10
Bladestorm
Cooldown: 110 seconds
Cause a Bladestorm of destructive force around Samuro for 4 seconds, making him Unstoppable and dealing 235 damage per second to nearby enemies.
Illusion Master
Cooldown: 8 seconds
Mirror Images can be controlled individually or as a group and their damage is increased by 100%.
Passive: Image Transmission's cooldown is reduced to 10 seconds.
13
Mirrored Steel
Samuro's Basic Attacks against Heroes reduce the cooldown of Mirror Image by 1 second.
Kawarimi
Wind Walk creates an Image at Samuro's location that will continue whatever he was doing.
  • If there are already two Images active, the one farthest from Samuro will be destroyed.
Shukuchi
Wind Walk teleports Samuro a short distance in the direction he's currently facing.
Type: Movement: Teleport
Affects: Self
Targeting: No target
Range: 4
  • Samuro does not teleport, if he is Rooted, when Wind Walk is cast, but will be Stealthed normally.
16
Merciless Strikes
Samuro's Basic Attacks against Stunned, Rooted, or Slowed enemies are always Critical Strikes.
Type: Damage modifier
Affects: Self
  • These attacks benefits from all talents affecting Critical Strike.
  • Activated Critical Strike bonuses stack additively with Criticals from Merciless Strikes. Bonuses from Phantom Pain are added to both instances of Critical Strike.
Harsh Winds
Attacking a Hero during Wind Walk causes them to take 30% increased damage from Samuro for 3 seconds.
Type: Damage modifier: Debuff
Affects: Enemies
  • Stacks multiplicatively with other damage bonuses affecting Samuro's damage output
Press the Attack
While Advancing Strikes is active, Samuro and his Mirror Images gain 10% Attack Speed every time they Basic Attack, up to 40%.
20
Dance of Death
Mirror Images use Bladestorm when Samuro does, but for 25% damage.
Three Blade Style
Samuro's Mirror Images gain an additional 50% of his Health and last up to 36 seconds.
Wind Strider
Wind Walk's cooldown is reduced by 6 seconds.
Blademaster's Pursuit
Advancing Strikes grants an additional 15% Movement Speed for Samuro and his Mirror Images, and its duration is increased by 2 seconds.
