Longboat Raid!
Cooldown: 90 seconds
Hop into an Unstoppable Longboat that fires at nearby enemies for 112 damage per second and can fire a mortar that deals 205 damage in an area. The boat has increased Health for each Viking inside. If the boat is destroyed by enemies, all Vikings are Stunned for 1.5 seconds. Lasts 15 seconds.
Requires all surviving Vikings to be nearby.
Type: Summon
Targeting: No target
Properties: Stasis
Cast time: Instant
  • Longboat Raid! cannot be cast unless all alive Vikings are within 20 radius.
  • While Longboat Raid! is active, Vikings inside are removed from the battlefield and put in Stasis, removing most debuffs and making them immune to damage.
