Skills
Nature's Toxin
***Passive:*** Your Basic Attacks and damaging Abilities poison their target, dealing 30 (+5% per level) damage a second for 3 seconds. Every additional application increases the duration by 3 seconds, up to a maximum of 9 seconds.
  • Damage is dealt 1 tick per second.
Q
Noxious Blossom
60 Mana *Cooldown 8 seconds*
After 0.5 seconds, cause an area to explode with pollen dealing 160 (+4% per level) damage.
W
Crippling Spores
50 Mana *Cooldown 10 seconds*
Enemies currently afflicted by Nature's Toxin have its duration increased by 3 seconds and are slowed by 40% decaying over 3 seconds.
  • Only activatable, if there is an enemy afflicted by Nature's Toxin.
  • Has global range.
E
Wisp
*Cooldown 30 seconds*
Spawn a Wisp to scout an area. Can be redirected once active. When the Wisp is in a bush for more 2 seconds, its vision radius is increased by 75%. Lasts 45 seconds.
  • Wisp requires two instances of damage to be killed.
  • You can only have one Wisp at a time.
  • Cooldown starts when the ablility is first cast.
E
Relocate Wisp
*Cooldown 1 seconds*
Orders your Wisp to move to a new location.
  • Replaces Wisp while your Wisp is alive.

R
Leaping Strike

Lunara moves 20% faster by leaping short distances.
