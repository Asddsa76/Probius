Double Back

Bomb's Away

❢ Reward: After damaging 20 Heroes, the range and speed of Cho's Rune Bomb is increased by 20%.

Edge of Madness

Double Trouble

❢ Reward: After hitting 20 Heroes, Shadowflame's cooldown is instead permanently reduced by 0.5 seconds.
