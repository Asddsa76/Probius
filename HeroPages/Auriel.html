Skills

D
Bestow Hope
Cooldown: 1.5 seconds
Passive: 60% of the damage you deal to Heroes and 20% dealt to non-Heroes is stored as energy. Bestow an allied Hero with Hope. While they remain near you, damage they deal causes you to gain energy. You can only have Bestow Hope on 1 ally at a time. You can store up to 475 energy.
Type: Resource
Scaling: 4.00% (max energy)
Affects: Allied Heroes
Targeting: Unit target
Cast time: Instant
Range: 10
Collect radius: 20
Q
Sacred Sweep
Cooldown: 8 seconds
Sweep the area with sacred power, dealing 45 damage to enemies and an additional 180 damage to enemies caught in the center.
Type: Damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping
Cast time: 0+0.625 seconds
Radius: 7
Arc: 180 degrees
Center arc: 30 degrees
  • Enemies hit by center take both center and edge damage.
  • Center damage is dealt to all targets simultaneously once the cast fully finishes.
W
Ray of Heaven
Cooldown: 4 seconds
Consume your stored energy and heal allied Heroes in the area for the amount of energy consumed.
Type: Healing
Scaling: -
Affects: Allied Heroes
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect
Cast time: Instant
Range: 8
Radius: 3

Detainment Strike
Cooldown: 12 seconds
Deal 55 damage to the first enemy Hero hit and knock them back. If they collide with terrain, they are also stunned for 1.25 seconds and take an additional 165 damage.
Type: Damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping, Knockback, Skillshot, Stun (conditional)
Cast time: 0+0.25 seconds
Range: 6
Hitbox: 1x2
Projectile speed: 30
Knockback duration: 0.25 seconds
Knockback speed: 25

Resurrect
Cooldown: 80 seconds
Channel on the spirit of a dead ally for 0.5 seconds. After a 5 second delay, they are brought back to life with 50% of their maximum Health at the location where they died.
Type: -
Affects: Allied Heroes
Targeting: Unit target
Properties: Channeled cast (stationary)
Cast time: 0.5+0 seconds
Range: 7
  • Interrupting the ability during its cast puts it on a 10-second cooldown.

Crystal Aegis
Cooldown: 60 seconds

Type: Damage / Status effect
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies / Allied Heroes, Self
Targeting: Unit target
Properties: Area of Effect, Stasis
Cast time: Instant
