Vault of the Wardens
Cooldown: 13 seconds
Leap into the air, becoming immune to all hostile effects for 0.75 seconds.
Type: Status effect
Affects: Self
Targeting: No target
Properties: Protected, Unstoppable
Cast time: Instant
  • Vault of the Wardens makes Maiev untargetable by skillshots and AoE for its duration.
  • Unit targeted Abilities and Basic Attacks can target and hit Maiev during Vault of the Wardens.
  • Maiev is able to move normally, but cannot attack or use her abilities during Vault of the Wardens.

55 Mana Cooldown: 14 seconds

If a tethered Hero moves too far from Maiev, they are pulled towards her, dealing 110 damage and breaking the tether.
Type: Attack modifier / Spell damage, Crowd Control
Scaling: - / 4.00%
Affects: Self / Enemy Heroes
Targeting: No target / Primed
Properties: Pull, Splash, Stun
Cast time: Instant

Tether:5.125
Arc: 220 degrees (cleave)
Pull distance: 6
Pull duration: 0.25 seconds
  • Cleave damages all enemies, but only Heroes are tethered.
  • Affected Heroes are always pulled a set distance upon tether breaking, regardless of Maiev's position.
  • Pulled Heroes are Stunned for 0.25 seconds after the pull ends.
  • Cooldown starts once the Ability is triggered by attacking an enemy.

Warden's Cage
80 Mana Cooldown: 100 seconds
Summon 8 Warden Avatars as a cage around Maiev. After 1.5 seconds, enemy Heroes that come in contact with an Avatar consume it and are knocked to the center of the cage.
Warden Avatars last 5 seconds.
Type: Crowd control
Affects: Enemy Heroes
Targeting: No target
Properties: Knockback
Cast time: Instant
Area of Effect: Circle
Radius: 5.5
  • Warden avatars have no collision.
  • Affected Heroes are pushed until they are within 0.1 range of the cage's center.
