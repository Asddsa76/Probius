Wrecking Ball
65 Mana Cooldown: 16 seconds
Throw a nearby enemy Hero, Minion, or Mercenary to the target location, dealing 91 damage to enemies near the impact and Slowing them by 30% for 2.5 seconds.
Type: Damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target / Automatic
Properties: Clamping
Impact - Area of Effect, Slow
Throw - Displacement, Stun
Cast time: Instant
Range: 5.5
Target search radius: 2.5
Impact radius: 2.5
Throw duration: 0.3125 seconds
  • Automatically selects the enemy closest to Garrosh to be thrown. Does not prioritise Heroes.
  • Enemy Structures and summons (that are not Heroic Pets) can be damaged, but not thrown.
  • The thrown enemy is briefly Stunned, interrupting channeled abilities, and then Dazed for the remainder of the flight.
