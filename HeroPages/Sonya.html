Skills

Fury
Use Fury instead of Mana, which is gained by taking or dealing damage. Using a Basic or Heroic Ability grants 10% Movement Speed for 4 seconds.
    Type: Resource
    Affects: Self
  • Sonya starts at 0 Fury when she respawns.
  • Basic Attacks generate 6 Fury each and every instance of damage Sonya takes generates 2 Fury.
Q
Ancient Spear
Cooldown: 13 seconds
Throw out a spear that pulls you to the first enemy hit, dealing 173 damage and briefly Stunning them. If this hits an enemy, generate 40 Fury.
    Type: Damage / Mobility: Launch
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Clamping, Skillshot, Stun
    Cast time: 0.15+0 seconds
    Range: 8.4
    Hitbox: 1.25x2.25
W
Seismic Slam
25 Fury Cooldown: 1 seconds
Deals 176 damage to the target enemy, and 44 to enemies behind the target.
    Type: Damage
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Unit target
    Properties: Splash
    Cast time: 0.15+0 seconds
    Range: 2.25
    Splash width: 3
    Splash length: 6
E
Whirlwind
40 Fury Cooldown: 5 / 0.75 seconds
Deals 126 damage per second to nearby enemies for 3 seconds, healing for 25% of damage dealt. Healing tripled versus Heroes.
    Type: Damage / Healing
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Mobile channel
    Cast time: Instant
    Radius: 2.5
    Tickrate: 2/s (x6)
    Damage per tick: 63
    Reactivate: Cancel Whirlwind - Cancels the Whirlwind ability.
R
Leap
Cooldown: 70 / 1.5 seconds
Leap into the air, dealing 135 damage to nearby enemies, and stunning them for 1.25 seconds.
    Type: Damage / Mobility: Launch
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping Stun
    Cast time: Instant
    Range: 10
    Impact radius: 3
    Leap duration: 0.25 seconds
R
Wrath of the Berserker
Cooldown: 60 seconds
Increase damage dealt by 40%. Reduce the duration of Stuns, Slows, and Roots against you by 50%. Lasts 15 seconds, and extends by 1 second for every 10 Fury gained.
    Type: Damage modifier: Buff
    Affects: Self
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: -
    Cast time: Instant
  • Cooldown starts when the ability is cast.
