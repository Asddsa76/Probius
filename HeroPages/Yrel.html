Skills

D
Divine Purpose
Cooldown: 8 seconds
Activate to instantly charge Yrel's next Basic Ability at no mana cost.
Passive: Yrel's Basic Abilities charge up over 1.5 seconds, increasing in effectiveness, but reducing Yrel's Movement Speed by 25%.
    Type: -
    Affects: Self
    Targeting: No target
    Cast time: Instant
  • Does not dismount on activation.
  • Yrel can channel Abilities indefinitely.
  • If Yrel is interrupted during channeling a Basic Ability, it goes on full cooldown without firing off.
Q
Vindication
65 Mana Cooldown: 6 seconds
Unleash holy energy around Yrel, dealing 42 damage to nearby enemies and healing her for 96.
Charging up this Ability increases its damage up to 140, and healing up to 320.
    Type: Damage / Healing
    Scaling: 4.00% (both)
    Affects: Enemies / Self
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Channeled cast (mobile)
    Cast time: Ch+0.1875 seconds
    Radius: 4
W
Righteous Hammer
65 Mana Cooldown: 16 seconds
Swing Yrel's hammer, dealing 38 damage to enemies in front of her and knocking them away.
Charging up this Ability increases its knockback distance, and damage up to 125. Enemies hit at maximum charge are Stunned for 0.75 seconds.
    Type: Damage
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Channeled cast (mobile), Clamping, Knockback, Stun (conditional)
    Cast time: Ch+0.125+0.1875 seconds
    Radius: 4
    Arc: 180 degrees
    Swing direction: Clockwise
    Knockback distance (min): 1.75
    Knockback distance (max): 6
    Knockback duration (min): 0.125 seconds
    Knockback duration (max): 0.25 seconds
E
Avenging Wrath
65 Mana Cooldown: 6 seconds
Leap to a location, dealing 225 damage to enemies in an area and Slowing them by 50% for 1 second.
Charging up this Ability increases its range.
    Type: Damage / Mobility: Launch
    Scaling: 4.00% (both)
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Channeled cast (mobile), Clamping, Slow, Self-castable
    Cast time: Ch+0.1875 seconds
    Range (min): 2.5
    Range (max): 8
    Radius: 2.5
    Leap duration: 0.625 seconds
R
Ardent Defender
40 Mana Cooldown: 120 seconds
Surround Yrel in a barrier for 3 seconds, absorbing all damage taken and healing her for 50% of the damage received.
    Type: Healing
    Scaling: -
    Affects: Self
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Absorbing
    Cast time: Instant
R
Sacred Ground
40 Mana Cooldown: 100 seconds
Yrel sanctifies the ground around her, gaining 50 Armor until she leaves the area.
    Type: Damage modifier: Armor
    Affects: Self
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Area of Effect
    Cast time: 0+0.1875 seconds
    Radius: 5.5

Talents

1
Light of Karabor
Increase Vindication's radius by 15%. Hitting an enemy Hero increases Vindication's healing by 40%. If more than one Hero is hit, the bonus is increased by 80%.
    Type: Healing modifier
    Affects: Ability
Dauntless
Casting a Basic Ability grants Yrel 50 Physical Armor for 2 seconds.
    Type: Damage modifier: Physical Armor
    Affects: Self
  • Requires successfully casting a Basic Ability.
  • Subsequent instances instances of Dauntless refresh the duration. Does not stack.
Maraad's Insight
After damaging an enemy hero with a Basic Ability, Yrel's next Basic Attack heals her for 128.
    Type: Attack modifier / Healing
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Self
  • Requires successfully casting a Basic Ability.
  • Maraad's Insight does not stack.
  • A basic attack at full health does not consume healing.

    Type: Attack Modifier / Damage modifier: Buff

  • Requires successfully casting a Basic Ability.
  • Holy Wrath does not stack.
