Skills

D
Salvager
Destroyed enemy Structures and your Rock-it! Turrets drop Scrap. Collecting Scrap restores 30 Mana and causes your Abilities to recharge three times as fast over 3 seconds.
Activate Salvager to dismantle a target Rock-it! Turret and turn it into Scrap.
Type: Destroy
Affects: Turrets
Targeting: Unit target
Cast time: Instant
Range: 7
  • Scrap spawns globally and persists on ground for 15 seconds.
  • Each Scrap collected increases the duration of the buff by 3 seconds.
  • Affects both Heroic and Basic Abilities' cooldowns, but not cooldowns from Abilities gained from Talents.
  • All Structures including Walls drop 1 Scrap.
  • Activating Salvager does not dismount.
  • Cast range matches Turrets' attack range.

W
Deth Lazor
60 Mana Cooldown: 12 / 0.5 seconds
Charged attack that deals 137 damage to enemies in a line. Damage and range increase the longer the Ability is charged, up to 100% after 3 seconds.
Deth Lazor can be channeled indefinitely.
Type: Spell damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping, Stationary channel
Cast time: 0.15+Ch+0.15 seconds
Area of Effect: Rectangle
Width:
- Initial: 1.0
- Max: 5.0
Height:
- Initial: 8.75
- Max: 16.5
  • Deth Lazor's distance increases by 0.75 every 0.25 seconds.
  • Width & base damage are increased every 1.5 seconds first to 2.0 and 205 damage at 1.5 second mark and then to 5.0 and 274 at 3 second mark.
  • Gazlowe can cancel Deth Lazor by issuing a stop or move command. Canceling the ability this way doesn't consume Mana nor put it on cooldown.
  • Being Stunned or Silenced during channeling causes Gazlowe to fire Deth Lazor in the direction he's currently facing, while Knockbacks may suppress the cast.

Type: Spell damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
  • Turret Deth Lazors do not have their damage or width increased with channeling, only the range, which matches Gazlowe's Lazor.
  • All lazors are fired towards the target point.
  • Turret lazors only benefit from charge speed increase of Hyperfocus Coils talent.

Type: Active
Targeting: No target
Cast time: Instant
  • Each piece of Scrap drops in one of the 12 possible locations in 3.5 radius around Gazlowe.
  • Scrap cannot drop in the same location more than once during the same cast.
