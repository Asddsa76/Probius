Oracle
Cooldown: 30 seconds
Activate to greatly increase Tassadar's vision radius, allow him to see over obstacles, and detect stealthed units. Lasts for 5 seconds.
Type: Status effect / Vision
Affects: Enemies / Self
Targeting: No target
Properties: Area of Effect, Revealed
Cast time: Instant
Area of Effect: Circle
Radius: 20

Distortion Beam
Tassadar's Basic Attack is a Distortion Beam that slows enemy units by 25%.
Type: Attack modifier, Crowd control
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: No target
Properties: Slow
Q
Plasma Shield
50 Mana Cooldown: 8 seconds
Grant an allied Hero, Minion or Structure a Shield that absorbs 455 damage over 4 seconds. If the target is a Hero, they heal for 40% of their Basic Attack damage done while Shielded.
Type: Attack modifier / Shield / Healing
Scaling: 4.00% (shield)
Affects: Enemies / Allied Heroes, Minions & Structures, Self / Allied Heroes, Self
Targeting: Unit target
Properties: Lifesteal
Cast time: Instant
Range: 11
  • Lifesteal is granted from all targets damaged by Distortion Beam and Archon AoE attacks.
W
Psionic Storm
50 Mana Cooldown: 8 / 0.5 seconds
Deal 82 damage per second to enemies in target area for 3 seconds. Damage increases by 12% for each consecutive instance of damage, up to 60%.
Type: Spell damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect
Cast time: 0.1+0 seconds
Range: 9
Area of Effect: Circle
Radius: 2.5
Tickrate: 2/s
Damage per tick: 41
  • Each target has their own damage multiplier counter.
  • Damage multiplier stacks multiplicatively with Spell Power and other damage modifiers affecting Tassadar's Spell damage output.
E
Dimensional Shift
75 Mana Cooldown: 30 / 0.5 seconds
Tassadar becomes Invulnerable and Unrevealable for 2 seconds. While shifted, he has 25% increased Movement Speed.
Type: Status effect
Affects: Self
Targeting: No target
Properties: Stasis, Stealth, Unrevealable
Cast time: Instant
R
Archon
80 Mana Cooldown: 100 seconds
Tassadar transforms into an Archon and gains a Plasma Shield. His Basic Attacks deal 158 damage, slow the target by 30% for 1 second and splash for 79 damage to enemies within 2.5 range. Lasts for 10 seconds.
Passive: Archon refreshes the cooldown of Dimensional Shift.
Type: Attack modifier, Crowd control / Bonus damage, Shield
Scaling: 4.00% (damage)
Affects: Enemies / Self
Targeting: No target
Properties: Slow, Splash
Cast time: Instant
Attack damage: 158
Attack speed: 1.00
Splash damage: 79
Splash radius: 2.5

Force Wall
25 Mana Cooldown: 8 seconds
Create a wall that blocks all units from moving through it for 2 seconds.
Passive: Increases the slow amount of Distortion Beam to 30%.
Type: Terrain object
Affects: All units
Targeting: Point target
Cast time: 0.1+0 seconds
Range: 10
Area of Effect: Rectangle
Width: 9

Talents

1
Psi-Infusion
❢ Quest: Gain 1 Mana for every non-Structure enemy hit by Psionic Storm.
❢ Reward: After hitting 500 enemies, increase the size of Psionic Storm by 20%.
❢ Reward: After hitting 1000 enemies, increase the damage of Psionic Storm by 20%.
Type: Damage modifier
Affects: Ability
Bonus radius: 0.5
  • Stacks multiplicatively with innate damage bonus, Spell Power and other damage modifiers affecting Tassadar's Spell damage output.
Templar's Will
❢ Quest: Attacking enemy Heroes restores 8 Mana per second.
❢ Reward: After spending 30 seconds attacking Heroes, Distortion Beam's damage is increased by 125%.
❢ Reward: After spending 60 seconds attacking Heroes, Distortion Beam's range is increased by an additional 1.
Type: Damage modifier
Affects: Self
  • Each tick of Distortion Beam grants 0.25 seconds of quest progress and 2 Mana for all targets. Archon Basic Attacks grant 1 second of quest progress and 8 Mana from their primary target.
  • Archon Basic Attacks are unaffected by either quest reward.
  • Stacks additively with other damage modifiers affecting Tassadar's Physical damage output.
Khaydarin Resonance
❢ Quest: Regeneration Globes grant an additional 50% Mana.
❢ Reward: After collecting 10 Regeneration Globes, Plasma Shield's value is increased by 15%.
❢ Reward: After collecting 20 Regeneration Globes, Plasma Shield's value is increased by 30%.
Type: Bonus Shield
Scaling: -
Affects: Ability
