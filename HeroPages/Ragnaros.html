Molten Core
*Cooldown 120 seconds*
Channel on an allied or destroyed Fort or Keep to replace it with Ragnaros's ultimate form, temporarily gaining new Abilities, having 3996 Health that burns away over 18 seconds. Ragnaros returns to his normal form upon losing all Health in Molten Core.
Type: Transformation
Scaling: 4.00% (health & decay)
Affects: Allied or destroyed Forts & Keeps
Targeting: Unit target
Properties: Channeled cast, Heroic Target, Stasis
Cast time: 2(ch)+0 seconds
Range: 5.75
Health decay: 222 per second
  • Interrupting Molten Core during its cast puts it on 10 second cooldown.
  • Entering Molten Core puts real Ragnaros in a special Stasis, removing most debuffs and making him untargetable and immune to damage until Molten Core ends.
  • Ragnaros can exit Molten Core after a 1 second channel by reactivating the Trait. Exiting can be cancelled by pressing the Trait button again before channel is complete.
  • Ragnaros cannot be targeted by friendly abilities during Molten Core, but buffs and HoTs applied before hand persist for their duration.
  • Molten Core Ragnaros cannot be targeted or affected by Abathur's Ultimate Evolution, Sylvanas' Black Arrow disable or Zagara's Devouring Maw.
