Skills

Shrike
Basic Attacks apply a Dose to enemies, dealing an additional 40 damage over 5 seconds, and stacking up to 5 times. Every 0.5 seconds, Ana is healed for 50% of the damage dealt by Shrike.
Aim Down Sights
Shrike can be activated to increase the Range of Healing Dart and Sleep by 25%, allowing them to pierce one Hero but also reduce Movement Speed by 25%. Lasts until canceled.
    Type: Attack Modifier / Damage / Healing
    Scaling: 4.00% (damage) / - (healing)
    Affects: Enemies / Self
    Properties: Damage over time, Lifesteal
    Tickrate: 2 / s (x10)
    Damage per tick: 4 (per stack)
Q
Healing Dart
10 Mana Cooldown: 2 seconds
Fire a dart which heals the first allied Hero hit for 195 Health. Does not affect full Health Heroes.
    Type: Healing
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Allied Heroes
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Clamping, Skillshot
    Cast time: 0+0.0625 seconds
    Range: 12.75
    Hitbox: 1x3.125
    Projectile speed: 50
W
Biotic Grenade
50 Mana Cooldown: 16 seconds
Toss a Grenade at the target area. Allied Heroes hit are are healed for 152 Health and receive 25% increased healing from Ana for 4 seconds. Enemies hit take 60 damage and receive 100% less healing for 2 seconds.
    Type: Damage / Healing / Healing modifier
    Scaling: 4.00% (both)
    Affects: Enemies / Allied Heroes, Self / Allied & Enemy Heroes, Self
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Area of Effect
    Cast time: 0.125+0 seconds
    Range: 12
    Radius: 2.25
E
Sleep Dart
50 Mana Cooldown: 14 seconds
Fire a dart that puts the first enemy Hero hit to Sleep, rendering them unable to act for 3 seconds. Sleep's effects end instantly if the target takes damage after the first 0.5 seconds.
    Type: Crowd Control
    Affects: Enemy Heroes
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Clamping, Skillshot, Sleep
    Cast time: 0.25+0 seconds
    Range: 11.75
    Hitbox: 0.5x1.25
    Projectile speed: 24
  • Damage over time effects applied beforehand do not interrupt Sleep
R
Nano Boost
60 Mana Cooldown: 70 seconds
Instantly boost an allied Hero, restoring 200 Mana. For the next 8 seconds, they gain 30% Spell Power and their Basic Ability cooldowns recharge 150% faster.
Cannot be used on Ana.
    Type: Damage modifier: Spell Power
    Affects: Allied Heroes
    Targeting: Unit target
    Properties: -
    Cast time: 0.125+0 seconds
    Range: 8
R
Eye of Horus
45 Mana Cooldown: 60 seconds
Assume a sniping position, gaining the ability to fire up to 6 specialized rounds with unlimited range. Rounds pierce allied and enemy Hero but collide with enemy Structure in their path. Allies are healed for 225 and enemies are damaged for 135. Deals 50% less damage to Structures.
Ana is unable to move while Eye of Horus is active.
    Type: -
    Scaling: -
    Affects: -
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Stationary channel
    Channel time: Infinite
    Cast time: 0.0625+Ch+0
    Reactivate:
    - Q&R: Eye of Horus - High Powered Round
    - W&E: Eye of Horus Cancel - Cancels Eye of Horus
  • Ana cannot move, Mount, Hearth or use other abilities while channeling Eye of Horus.
  • Channel will end only if interrupted, manually cancelled or once all shots have been fired.
  • If Eye of Horus is cancelled before any shots are fired, it will not be put on cooldown.
Q
Eye of Horus - High Powered Round
Cooldown: 1 seconds
Fire a specialized round with unlimited range, piercing all allied and enemy Heroes in its path. Allies are healed for 285 and enemies are damaged for 166. Deals 50% less damage to Structures.
    Type: Damage / Healing
    Scaling: 4.00% (both)
    Affects: Allied & Enemy Heroes, Enemy Structures
    Targeting: Point target / Target search (closest)
    Properties: Skillshot
    Cast time: Instant
    Range: Global
    Width: 1
  • Can be activated using Q, R or left mouse button during Eye of Horus.
  • Using High Powered Round briefly provides vision around the line of fire and upon hitting around the target.

Armored Stance
Increase Shrike's damage by 50%. While Aim Down Sights is active, gain 25 Armor.
Dynamic Optics
