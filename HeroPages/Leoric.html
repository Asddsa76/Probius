Skills

Undying
Leoric becomes a ghost when he dies, and resurrects upon reaching full Health. Leoric deals no damage while dead.
    Type: Special death state
    Affects: Self
    Properties: Untargetable by everything
  • Leoric can use Ghastly Swing (Q), Drain Essence (W) and Hearth and provide vision while Undying. Leoric cannot use other abilities or interact with other units in any other way while Undying, unless otherwise noted.
  • Reaching full health while Undying automatically resurrects at his current location after 1 second. Leoric is still Undying, but unable to act for the duration of the resurrection sequence.

Wrath Of The Bone King
Leoric's first two Basic Attacks cleave for 100% damage, and his third Basic Attack deals 200% damage to a single target.
    Type: Attack modifier / Damage modifier
    Affects: Self
    Properties: Splash
    Cleave radius: 3.5
    Cleave arc: 220 degrees
Q
Skeletal Swing
80 Mana Cooldown: 14 seconds
Leoric swings his mace, dealing 150 damage and Slowing enemies by 40% for 2.5 seconds. If an enemy Hero is hit, refund 50% of the cooldown and Mana cost. Peasants!
    Type: Damage
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping, Slow
    Cast time: 0.15+0.1875 seconds
    Radius: 5
    Arc: 180 degrees
Q
Ghastly Swing
Cooldown: 8 seconds
Slow enemies by 40% for 2.5 seconds.
    Type: Non-damaging
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping, Slow
    Cast time: 0.15+0.1875 seconds
    Radius: 5
    Arc: 180 degrees
  • Does not benefit from talents affecting Skeletal Swing.
W
Drain Hope
60 Mana Cooldown: 10 seconds
Grab an enemy Hero's soul, dealing up to 20% of their maximum Health as damage and healing Leoric for up to 20% of his maximum Health while he is nearby, over 4 seconds. Leoric is Slowed by 20% while this is active.
    Type: Damage / Healing
    Scaling: -
    Affects: Enemy Heroes / Self
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Clamping, Percent damage, Skillshot, Self-Slow
    Cast time: 0.15+0 seconds
    Range: 8
    Hitbox: 1.25x1.5
    Tickrate: 2 / second (x8)
W
Drain Essence
Cooldown: 9 seconds
Throw out a chain, attaching to the first enemy Hero hit, healing Leoric for up to 10% of his maximum Health over 4 seconds as long as the enemy remains close.
    Type: Healing
    Scaling: -
    Affects: Enemy Heroes
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Clamping, Skillshot
    Cast time: 0.15+0 seconds
    Range: 8
    Hitbox: 1.25x1.5
    Tickrate: 2 / second (x8)
  • Does not benefit from talents affecting Drain Hope.
E
Wraith Walk
50 Mana Cooldown: 14 / 0.25 seconds
Separate from your body, becoming Unstoppable and gaining Movement Speed accelerating up to 50% over 2.5 seconds. When Wraith Walk ends or is canceled, your body jumps to your wraith.
    Type: Mobility: Teleport
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Unstoppable
    Cast time: Instant
    Reactivate: Cancel Wraith Walk - Cancels Wraith Walk
  • Leoric's ghost is an invulnerable summon that moves at regular Movement speed.
  • Ghost's Movement speed can be affected by conveyor belts in Volskaya Foundry.
  • Unaffected by Roots.
  • Unstoppable is removed before teleporting to ghost.
R
Entomb
60 Mana Cooldown: 50 seconds
Create an unpathable tomb in front of you for 4 seconds.
    Type: Terrain object
    Affects: All units
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Clamping
    Cast time: 0.0625+0.875 seconds
    Inner length: 7
    Outer length: 8
    Inner width: 4
    Outer width: 6.5
    Delay: 0.1875 seconds
R
March of the Black King
100 Mana Cooldown: 80 seconds
Become Unstoppable and swing your mace three times, healing yourself for 12% of your maximum Health for each enemy Hero hit and dealing 250 damage.
    Type: Damage / Healing / Mobility: Dash
    Scaling: 4.00% (damage)/ - (healing)
    Affects: Enemy Heroes / Self
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping, Unstoppable
    Cast time: 0+2.5 seconds
    Range: 8.125
    Swing radius: 2.5
    Swing arc: 270 degrees
  • Swings take place at 0.31/1.31/2.31 seconds (or distancewise at 1/4.25/7.5).
