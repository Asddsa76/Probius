Skills
Undying
Leoric becomes a ghost when he dies, and resurrects upon reaching full Health. Leoric deals no damage while dead.
    Type: Special death stateAffects: SelfProperties: Untargetable by everything
  • Leoric can use Ghastly Swing (Q), Drain Essence (W) and Hearth and provide vision while Undying. Leoric cannot use other abilities or interact with other units in any other way while Undying, unless otherwise noted.
  • Reaching full health while Undying automatically resurrects at his current location after 1 second. Leoric is still Undying, but unable to act for the duration of the resurrection sequence.
