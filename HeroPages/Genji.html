Skills
Cyber Agility
*Cooldown 15 seconds*
Activate to jump to target area.
  • Can go over walls and terrain.
Q
Shuriken
20 Mana *Cooldown 12 seconds*
Throw 3 Shuriken in a spread pattern, each dealing 65 (+4% per level) damage to the first enemy hit.Stores up to 3 charges. Shuriken's cooldown replenishes all charges at the same time.
  • There is 0.5 second cooldown inbetween charges.
W
Deflect
70 Mana *Cooldown 16 seconds*
Channel for 1.25 seconds, becoming Protected and blocking damage. Any damage blocked while channeling causes Genji to throw a Kunai toward the nearest enemy, prioritizing Heroes and dealing 55 (+4% per level) damage.
  • Damage over time effects and Abilities that deal periodic damage proc Deflect on every tick of damage.
  • If the channel gets interrupted by a Stun or a Silence, the damage of the Ability that caused the interruption still gets deflected.
E
Swift Strike
75 Mana *Cooldown 12 seconds*
Dash forward, dealing 190 (+4% per level) damage to all enemies in a line. Enemy Heroes that die within 1.5 seconds of being hit with Swift Strike cause the cooldown and mana cost to be refunded.
  • Can go over walls and terrain. Trying to dash into a middle of terrain ends the dash at the edge of terrain closest to destination point.
R
Dragonblade
75 Mana *Cooldown 100 seconds*
Unleash the Dragonblade for 8 seconds. While active, Dragonblade can be reactivated to lunge forward and slash in a huge arc, dealing 240 (+4% per level) damage.
  • Dragonblade replaces Shuriken for its duration and can be reactivated with both Q and R keys
  • Genji is unable to reactivate Dragonblade if he is Silenced or Rooted.
R
X-Strike
90 Mana *Cooldown 80 seconds*
Perform two slashes dealing 135 (+4% per level) damage. The slashes detonate after 1.25 seconds causing an additional 270 (+4% per level) damage to enemies in their area.
  • Slashes are aligned 45 degrees to both sides from the direction Genji is facing, first slash hitting towards his right hand side. Genji turns before ability is cast.
  • For the duration of the slash animation, Genji is put in a special Stasis, removing most debuffs and making him untargetable and immune to damage.
  • Self-casting the ability makes Genji move forward a bit.
