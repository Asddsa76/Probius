Skills

Cyber Agility
Cooldown: 15 seconds
Activate to jump to target area.
  • Can go over walls and terrain.
Q
Shuriken
20 Mana Cooldown: 12 seconds
Throw 3 Shuriken in a spread pattern, each dealing 65 (+4% per level) damage to the first enemy hit.
Stores up to 3 charges. Shuriken's cooldown replenishes all charges at the same time.
  • There is 0.5 second cooldown inbetween charges.
W
Deflect
70 Mana Cooldown: 16 seconds
Channel for 1.25 seconds, becoming Protected and blocking damage. Any damage blocked while channeling causes Genji to throw a Kunai toward the nearest enemy, prioritizing Heroes and dealing 55 (+4% per level) damage.
  • Damage over time effects and Abilities that deal periodic damage proc Deflect on every tick of damage.
  • If the channel gets interrupted by a Stun or a Silence, the damage of the Ability that caused the interruption still gets deflected.
E
Swift Strike
75 Mana Cooldown: 12 seconds
Dash forward, dealing 190 (+4% per level) damage to all enemies in a line. Enemy Heroes that die within 1.5 seconds of being hit with Swift Strike cause the cooldown and mana cost to be refunded.
  • Can go over walls and terrain. Trying to dash into a middle of terrain ends the dash at the edge of terrain closest to destination point.
R
Dragonblade
75 Mana Cooldown: 100 seconds
Unleash the Dragonblade for 8 seconds. While active, Dragonblade can be reactivated to lunge forward and slash in a huge arc, dealing 240 (+4% per level) damage.
  • Dragonblade replaces Shuriken for its duration and can be reactivated with both Q and R keys
  • Genji is unable to reactivate Dragonblade if he is Silenced or Rooted.
R
X-Strike
90 Mana Cooldown: 80 seconds
Perform two slashes dealing 135 (+4% per level) damage. The slashes detonate after 1.25 seconds causing an additional 270 (+4% per level) damage to enemies in their area.
  • Slashes are aligned 45 degrees to both sides from the direction Genji is facing, first slash hitting towards his right hand side. Genji turns before ability is cast.
  • For the duration of the slash animation, Genji is put in a special Stasis, removing most debuffs and making him untargetable and immune to damage.
  • Self-casting the ability makes Genji move forward a bit.

Talents

1
Swift as the Wind
Hitting an enemy Hero with Swift Strike increases Genji's Movement Speed by 30% for 3 seconds.
Agile Dismount
While mounted, the range of Cyber Agility is increased by 70% and its cooldown is reduced by 10 seconds.
Pathfinder
Jumping over terrain with Cyber Agility increases Genji's Movement Speed by 25% for 6 seconds.
4
Shuriken Mastery
❢ Quest: Hit enemy Heroes with Shuriken.
❢ Reward: After hitting 35 Heroes, Shuriken damage is increased by 25.
❢ Reward: After hitting 75 Heroes, Cyber Ability now refunds 2 charges of Shuriken.
Strike At The Heart
Enemies hit by the end of Swift Strike take an additional 144 damage over 2 seconds.
Dragon Claw
Active: After Genji blocks 330 (+4% per level) damage with Deflect, he may activate Dragon Claw to deal 190 (+4% per level) damage to all nearby enemies.
7
Augmented Guard
When Deflect ends, Genji gains a Shield equal to 50% of the damage blocked for 4 seconds.
Perfect Defense
Deflected damage reduces the cooldown of Deflect by 2 seconds, up to a maximum of 10 seconds.
Dodge
Genji dodges 1 Heroic Basic Attack every 8 seconds.
Cyber Shield
Using Cyber Agility grants 50 Spell Armor for 2.5 seconds.
10
Dragonblade
Cooldown: 100 seconds
Unleash the Dragonblade for 8 seconds. While active, Dragonblade can be reactivated to lunge forward and slash in a huge arc, dealing 240 (+4% per level) damage.
X-Strike
Cooldown: 90 seconds
Perform two slashes dealing 135 (+4% per level) damage. The slashes detonate after 1.25 seconds causing an additional 270 (+4% per level) damage to enemies in their area.
13
Shingan
Hitting an enemy with all 3 Shuriken deals 120 bonus damage.
Flow Like Water
Each enemy Hero hit with Swift Strike reduces its cooldown by 3 seconds.
Double Jump
Cyber Agility stores 2 charges.
16
Reflect
Deflect also deals an additional 33% of the damage blocked.
  • All damage is dealt in Spell damage
  • Reflected damage is dealt before Armor reductions.
Final Cut
After 1 second, Swift Strike deals an additional 130 damage to all enemies in the area.
Steady Blade
Each enemy Hero hit by Swift Strike increases the damage of the next Swift Strike by 20%. This bonus stacks up to 3 times.
20
The Dragon Becomes Me
Each time Dragonblade hits an enemy Hero, the duration of Dragonblade is increased by 0.5 second. If an Hero is killed within 1.5 seconds of being hit by Dragonblade, Swift Strike's cooldown is reset.
Living Weapon
Each enemy Hero hit by X-Strike reduces its cooldown by 12 seconds.
  • Each individual slash applies cooldown reduction.
Sharpened Stars
Shuriken now pierce all enemies hit.
Zanshin
❢ Quest: Block 6500 damage with Deflect, including damage blocked so far.
❢ Reward: Deflect hits all nearby enemy Heroes.
