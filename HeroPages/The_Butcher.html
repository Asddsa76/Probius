Fresh Meat
Upon dying nearby enemy Minions drop 1 Fresh Meat and enemy Heroes drop 20 Fresh Meat. Fresh Meat can be picked up to gain 0.5 bonus Attack Damage per Meat. The Butcher loses 15 Fresh Meat upon dying.
❢ Quest: Collect 200 Fresh Meat.
❢ Reward: Gain an additional 125 Attack Damage and 25% increased Attack Speed. Heroes continue to drop 10 Fresh Meat, Minions no longer drop Fresh Meat, and Fresh meat is no longer lost on death.
Type: Bonus damage
Scaling: -
Affects: Self
Radius:
- Drop: 13.125 (Minions) / upon takedown (Heroes)
- Collect: 0.125
Duration: 15 seconds

Butcher's Brand
60 Mana Cooldown: 14 seconds
Deal 37 damage to an enemy and Brand them for 4 seconds. The Butcher's Basic Attacks against the Branded target heal him for 75% of the damage done.
Basic Attacks against Branded Heroes heal for double and extend the duration of the Brand by 0.5 seconds.
Type: Spell damage / Attack modifier (targets), Healing

Affects: Non-Structure enemies / Self
Targeting: Unit target

Ruthless Onslaught
50 Mana Cooldown: 15 seconds
Charge at an enemy, becoming Unstoppable and gaining Movement Speed. If The Butcher reaches the target, they are Stunned for 1 second and take 119 damage.
Type: Spell damage, Crowd control / Status effect
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Non-Structure enemies
Targeting: Unit target
Properties: Mobile channel, Unstoppable, Stun
Cast time: 0.5+ch+0 seconds
Range: 12
  • Charge can be manually cancelled by reactivating the ability. Otherwise The Butcher is uncontrollable during the charge.
  • Charge is automatically cancelled, if it has been active for 4 seconds without hitting the target or if the target becomes untargetable or moves past 5 units beyond its maximum range.

Can be cast while using Ruthless Onslaught.

Targeting: No target
Properties: Area of Effect

Lamb to the Slaughter
75 Mana Cooldown: 90 seconds
Throw a hitching post that attaches to the nearest enemy Hero after a 1 second delay. This deals 171 damage and causes the enemy to be chained to the post and Silenced for 3 seconds.
Type: Spell damage, Crowd control
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemy Heroes
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Knockback, Silence
Cast time: Instant
Range: 14
Area of Effect: Circle
Radius: 4 (search)
  • 'Nearest enemy' refers to the one closest to the center of the search area.
  • Chained enemies will be pulled to the center, if they try to escape the area.
