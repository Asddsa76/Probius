Flight
*Cooldown 75 seconds*
Instead of mounting, Falstad can fly a great distance over terrain.
Type: Movement: Launch
Properties: Channeled cast (stationary), Unstoppable, Untargetable
Cast time: 2.0(ch)+0 seconds
Range: 75
  • Cast can be cancelled by issuing a 'move' or 'stop' command or interrupted by enemy abilities that Stun, Silence, Stasis or Knockback. Taking damage does not interrupt the cast.
  • Interrupting the channel in any way puts Flight on a 10 second cooldoown.
