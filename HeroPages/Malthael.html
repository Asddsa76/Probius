Skills

Reaper's Mark
Basic Attacks cleave in an area in front of Malthael and afflict non-Structure targets with Reaper's Mark for 4 seconds. Marked enemies are revealed and take damage equal to 1.75% of their maximum Health every 1 second.
Type: Attack modifier / Damage
Scaling: -
Affects: Self / Non-Structure enemies
Properties: Percent damage, Revealed
Cleave radius: 3.25
Cleave arc: 169.9914 degrees
Q
Soul Rip
25 Mana Cooldown: 2 seconds
Extract the souls of nearby enemies afflicted by Reaper's Mark, dealing 100 (+4% per level) damage and healing Malthael for 25 (+4% per level) per target hit. Heroic targets heal Malthael for an additional 4% of the Hero's maximum Health.
Type: Damage / Healing
Scaling: 4.00% (damage) / 4.00% (healing vs. non-Heroes) / - (healing vs. Heroes)
Affects: Non-Structure enemies / Self
Targeting: No target
Cast time: Instant
Radius: 6.5
  • Can only be activated if there is at least one enemy affected by Reaper's Mark within the ability's radius.
W
Wraith Strike
30 Mana Cooldown: 5 seconds
Instantly teleport through an enemy afflicted by Reaper's Mark, dealing 59 (+4% per level) damage and refreshing Reaper's Mark.
Type: Damage / Mobility: Teleport
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Non-Structure enemies
Targeting: Unit target
Cast time: Instant
Radius: 6
  • Can only be activated if there is at least one enemy affected by Reaper's Mark.
  • Malthael teleports to the opposite side of the target from his location.
E
Death Shroud
50 Mana Cooldown: 8 seconds
After 0.25 seconds, unleash a wave of dark mist that applies Reaper's Mark to enemies it hits.
Type: Damage
Scaling: -
Affects: Non-Structure enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Clamping
Cast time: 0.25 seconds
Range: 11.25
Hitbox: 3x1.75
Projectile speed: 10

Tormented Souls
20 Mana Cooldown: 80 seconds
Unleash a torrent of souls, continually applying Reaper's Mark to nearby enemies for 4 seconds. When Tormented Souls is cast and when it expires, reset the cooldown of Wraith Strike.
Type: Damage
Scaling: -
Affects: Non-Structure enemies
Targeting: No target
Properties: Area of Effect
Cast time: 0.125 seconds
Radius: 5
R
Last Rites
100 Mana Cooldown: 65 seconds
Apply a death sentence to an enemy Hero that, after 2 seconds, deals damage equal to 50% of their missing Health.
❢ Repeatable Quest: Enemies killed between the application of Last Rites and within 1.5 seconds of it dealing damage permanently reduce its cooldown by 5 seconds, to a minimum of 15 seconds.
Type: Damage
Scaling: -
Affects: Non-Vehicle Enemy Heroes
Targeting: Unit target
Properties: Percent damage
Cast time: 0.125 seconds
Range: 5
  • Enemy Heroes damaged enough to be killed by using this Ability within 12 range are indicated by a red skull above them provided the Ability is not on cooldown.
