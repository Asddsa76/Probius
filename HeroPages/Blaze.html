Oil Spill
50 Mana Cooldown: 12 / 1.0 seconds
Vector Targeting
Dispense a slick of oil that lasts for 5 seconds and Slows enemies that come in contact with it by 50%.
Flame Stream can set Oil Spills on fire for 2.5 seconds, dealing 16 damage every 0.3 seconds to enemies inside. While on fire, Oil Spills no longer Slow enemies. Additionally, Blaze is Healed for 49 Health every 0.3 seconds while standing in flaming Oil Spills.
Stores up to 2 charges.
Type: Slow / Damage (burning) / Healing (burning)
Scaling: 4.00% / 4.00%
Affects: Enemies / Self
Targeting: Vector Targeting
Properties: Area of Effect
Cast time: 0.125 seconds
Range (initial point): 10
Width: 3
Length: 6
Damage per tick: 16 (x8)
Heal per tick: 39 (x8)
  • *) One Oil Spill consists of three smaller circular puddles with 1.5 radius, which centers are 1.5 units apart.
  • Each smaller oil puddle provides vision in 2.5 radius.
  • Lighting up an Oil Spill sets its remaining duration to 2.5 seconds regardless of how long it has been on the ground.
  • If no secondary direction is issued oil spill will be cast towards the direction Blaze is facing.
  • Self-casting creates an Oil Spill starting from Blaze's feet towards the direction he is facing.

Bunker Drop

Exiting the Bunker grants 25 Armor for 2 seconds. Bunkers last 10 seconds, or until destroyed.

  • Players can exit the Bunker to a direction of their choice, 1 second after entering. Exiting the Bunker requires you to target an area you have vision over.

Combustion

  • Interrupting Combustion in any way during its channel fires it with accumulated duration.
