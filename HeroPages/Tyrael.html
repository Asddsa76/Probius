Skills

Archangel's Wrath
Upon dying, become invulnerable and explode for 550 damage after 3.5 seconds.
    Type: Damage
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Special death state
    Radius: 4
  • Tyrael can move at normal speed, ignoring collision, until he explodes.
Q
El'Druin's Might
45 Mana Cooldown: 12 / 0.5 seconds
Throw El'Druin to the target area dealing 110 damage to nearby enemies and Slowing them by 25% for 2.5 seconds. It can be reactivated within 5 seconds to teleport Tyrael to El'Druin and Slow nearby enemies again.
    Type: Damage / Mobility: Teleport (reactivate)
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping, Slow
    Cast time: 0.15+0 seconds
    Range: 9
    Radius: 3
    Reactivate: El'Druin's Flash - Teleport to El'Druins might, slowing nearby enemies by 25% for 2 seconds.
  • El'Druin's Might cannot be cast on unpathable terrain.
  • Teleport on secondary cast has no range limit.
  • All reactivates of El'Druin's Might are considered Basic Ability casts.
W
Righteousness
45 Mana Cooldown: 9 seconds
Shields Tyrael for 336 damage and nearby allies for 40% as much for 4 seconds.
    Type: Shield
    Scaling: 4.00% (Self)/ - (Ally)
    Affects: Self / Non-Structure Allies
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Area of Effect
    Cast time: Instant
    Radius: 5
E
Smite
45 Mana Cooldown: 6 seconds
Rake target area for 150 damage. Allies moving through the targeted area gain 25% increased Movement Speed for 2 seconds.
    Type: Damage
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies / Allies
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping, Movement Speed buff
    Cast time: Instant
    Range: 5.5
    Area width: 7*
    Area length: 3*
  • *) Area consists of 5 overlapping circles with 1.5 radius in a wide V-shape.
  • The ground effect lingers for 3 seconds, applying the buff on the allies in the area every 0.25 seconds. Multiple instances of the buff only refresh the duration.
R
Judgment
80 Mana Cooldown: 70 seconds
After 0.75 seconds, charge an enemy Hero dealing 150 damage and stunning them for 1.5 seconds. Nearby enemies are knocked away and take 75 damage.
    Type: Damage / Mobility: Launch
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Unit target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Channeled cast (, Knockback, Stun, Unstoppable (self)
    Cast time: 0.75+0.35 seconds
    Range: 12
    Knockback radius: 5
    Knockback duration: 0.1875 seconds
    Knockback distance: 3.375
  • Interrupting Tyrael during the cast puts the ability on a 10-seconds cooldown.
  • Tyrael is Unstoppable during the charge.
R
Sanctification
75 Mana Cooldown: 100 seconds
After 0.5 seconds create a field of holy energy that makes allied Heroes Invulnerable. Lasts 3 seconds.
    Type: Invulnerable
    Affects: Self, Allied Heroes
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Channeled cast (stationary)
    Cast time: 0.5+0 seconds
    Radius: 6
  • Interrupting Tyrael during the cast puts the ability on a 10-seconds cooldown.

Talents

1
Salvation
Increase the Shield amount Tyrael gains from Righteousness by 25% and heal Tyrael for 140 Health if his Shield is destroyed. Does not increase the Shield amount gained by allies.
    Type: Healing
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Self
Justice for All
Incrase the Shield applied to allies by Righteousness to 75% of the Shield gained by Tyrael.
Ardent Restoration
Dealing damage to a Hero heals Tyrael for 18 over 5 seconds. Dealing damage resets this timer, and the heal amount stacks up to 10 times.
    Type: Heal over time
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Self
4
Stalwart Angel
Gain 25 Armor while El'druin's Might is active, and for 2 seconds after teleporting.
    Type: Damage modifier: Armor
    Affects: Self
Bound by Law
Increase El'druin's Might's Slow by 10%. Tyrael's Basic Attacks against enemies Slowed by El'druin's Migbht increase the Slow duration by 1 second, up to 4 seconds.
Divine Vigor
If Smite hits an enemy, Tyrael's Basic Attacks within the next 4 seconds heal for 60% of the damage dealt.
    Type: Attack modifier / Healing
    Scaling: -
    Affects: Self
7
Reciprocate
When Tyrael's Shield from Righteousness expires or is destroyed, it deals 170 damage to nearby enemies.
    Type: Damage
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Properties: Area of Effect
    Radius: 3
Purge Evil
Each enemy Hero hit by Smite increases Tyrael's Basic Attack damage by 55% for 4 seconds.
    Type: Attack Modifier / Damage modifier: Buff
    Affects: Self
Swift Retribution
Smite grants 20% more Movement Speed and also grants 25% Attack Speed for 2 seconds.
    Additional properties: Movement Speed buff, Attack Speed buff
10
Judgment
Cooldown: 70 seconds
After 0.75 seconds, charge an enemy Hero dealing 150 damage and Stunning them for 1.5 seconds. Nearby enemies are knocked away and take 75 damage.
Sanctification
Cooldown: 100 seconds
After 0.5 seconds create a field of holy energy that makes allied Heroes Invulnerable. Lasts 3 seconds.
13
Sword of Justice
Upon teleporting using El'druin's Might, El'druin swaps places with Tyrael, and El'druin's Might can be reactivated to teleport an additional time.
  • Reactivations do not increase the time El'Druin can stay on ground.
Holy Ground
Create a ring for 3 seconds that blocks enemies from entering the area teleported to using El'Druin's Might.
    Type: Terrain object
    Affects: Enemies
  • Displacement from the creation of unpathable terrain from Holy Ground doesn't interrupt channels.
Law and Order
Each enemy Hero hit by Smite reduces the cooldown of Righteousness by 1 second. Each allied Hero Shielded by Rigtheousness increases the damage of the next Smite by 25%.
    Type: Damage modifier
    Affects: Ability
16
Horadric Reforging
Tyrael's Basic Attacks reduce the cooldown of El'druin's Might by 1.5 seconds.
    Type: Attack modifier
    Affects: Ability
Burning Halo
Both Tyrael and El'druin deal 18 damage per second to nearby enemies. Teleporting increases this damage from Tyrael by 150% for 2 seconds.
    Type: Damage
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Properties: Area of Effect
    Radius: 3.5
    Tickrate: 1 / second
  • Enemies can take damage from both Tyrael's and El'Druins damage auras at the same time.
  • When Sword of Justice is picked, the first reactivation increases the damage around both Tyrael and El'Druin.
Smite the Wicked
While El'druin's Might is active, and for 2 seconds after teleporting, Smite's cooldown recharges 125% faster.
20
Angel of Justice
Increases the cast range of Judgment by 50%, and reduces the cooldown by 40 seconds.
Holy Arena
Increases duration of Sanctification by 1 second and increases the damage of allies by 25%.
    Type: Damage modifier:Buff
    Affects: Self, Allied Heroes
Defense of the Angels
Cooldown: 120 seconds
Activate to gain 50 Armor for 5 seconds.
Each time Righteousness's Shield absorbs damage, Defense of the Angel's cooldown is reduced by 3 seconds.
    Type: Damage modifier: Armor
    Affects: Self
Seal of El'druin
Using a Basic Ability grants 50% Attack Speed for 3 seconds.
