Skills

Lord Of Hatred
Hitting enemy Heroes with Basic Abilities reduces Mephisto's Basic Ability cooldowns. Skull Missile and Shade of Mephisto grant 1.5 seconds of cooldown reduction per Hero hit, and Lightning Nova grants 0.3 seconds per Hero hit.
Q
Skull Missile
20 Mana Cooldown: 8 seconds
Conjure a skull that travels in the target direction after 0.75 seconds, dealing 127 damage to enemies hit and Slowing Heroes hit by 25% for 2 seconds.
Type: Damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Skillshot, Slow, Uninterruptable
Cast time: 0.125 seconds
Range: 12.5
Hitbox: 2x1.8
Missile Speed: 17
W
Lightning Nova
50 Mana Cooldown: 14 seconds
A ring of lightning appears around Mephisto for 2.5 seconds. Enemies within the ring take 45 damage every 0.25 seconds. Each time a cast of Lightning Nova hits a Hero, its damage is increased by 3%, up to 30%.
Type: Damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: No target
Properties: Area of Effect, Uninterruptable
Cast time: 0.125 seconds
Inner range: 4.5
Outer range: 5.25
Damage per tick: 48
Tickrate: 4 / second (x11)
  • Mephisto's 4.5 Basic Attack Range is the same as the Lightning Nova Inner Radius, meaning that AA move puts him at the correct distance.
E
Shade of Mephisto
35 Mana Cooldown: 18 seconds
Teleport to a location, dealing 78 damage to nearby enemies and leaving behind a Shade of Mephisto at Mephisto's original location. After 2.5 seconds, Mephisto is teleported back to the Shade's location.
Type: Mobility: Teleport (primary cast) / Damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Self / Enemies
Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping, Self-castable, Uninterruptable
Cast time: 0.125 seconds
Range: 7.5
Radius: 2.5
  • The return always triggers regardless of any positive or negative status effects on Mephisto, with the exception of Stasis, which fully prevents relocation back to Shade's position.
  • Teleporting back dismounts, interrupts mounting, hearthing and channeling objectives, but does not interrupt channeling ultimates or Warhead Junction's Nukes.
R
Consume Souls
80 Mana Cooldown: 120 seconds
Channel for 2.5 seconds, revealing all enemy Heroes. After the Channel completes, all enemy Heroes take 357 damage and are Slowed by 25% for 2.5 seconds.
Type: Damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemy Heroes
Targeting: No target
Properties: Revealed, Slow, Channeled cast
Cast time: 2.5 seconds
Range: Global
R
Durance of Hate
50 Mana Cooldown: 50 seconds
After 1 second, unleash a wave of evil spirits that Root the first enemy Hero hit for 2.5 seconds and deal 250 damage to them over the same duration.
Durance of Hate spreads outwards from its initial target, Rooting and damaging additional nearby enemy Heroes.
Type: Damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemy Heroes
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Damage over Time, Root, Channeled cast
Cast time: 1 second
Range: 15
Hitbox: 2x1.25
Missile Speed: 17.5
Tickrate: 4 / second (x8)
Damage per tick: 20
Secondary target search radius: 6
  • If Mephisto teleports back with Shade of Mephisto during the cast time, the wave is unleashed in the direction of target point.

Talents

1
Unyielding Power
❢ Quest: Hit Heroes with Skull Missile.
❢ Reward: After hitting 20 Heroes, increase Skull Missile's damage by 100.
❢ Reward: After hitting 40 Heroes, Skull Missile's cooldown is permanently reduced to 5 seconds.
Type: Damage
Scaling: -
Affects: Ability
Furious Spark
Every 5th hit from Lightning Nova against the same target deals 95 bonus damage.
Type: Damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
  • Damage from Furious Spark does not benefit from Lightning Nova's innate damage multiplier nor create stacks for it.
Malicious Intent
Shade of Mephisto grants 20% Spell Power while active and for 2.5 seconds after ending.
Type: Damage modifier: Spell Power
Affects: Self
4
Hateful Mending
Skull Missile heals Mephisto for 60% of the damage it deals to Heroes.
Type: Lifesteal
Affects: Ability / Self
Static Barrier
At the end of its duration, Lightning Nova grants Mephisto a Shield equal to 40% of the damage it dealt to Heroes. Lasts 5 seconds.
Type: Shield
Affects: Ability / Self
  • Bonus damage from Furious Spark, Static Field, Aminosity and secondary nova from Mimic all contribute to shield amount gained.
  • Gaining a shield from Static Barrier while already having one causes them to stack and refreshes the duration.
Spite
Increase Regeneration Globe healing duration by 125%. Every tick of Regeneration Globe healing activates Lord of Hatred, reducing Basic Ability cooldowns by 0.75 seconds.
  • Makes Regen Globe tick 11 times, healing Mephisto for 19.8% of his maximum health over its duration.
7
Ghastly Armor
Shade of Mephisto grants 20 Armor while active and for 2.5 seconds after it ends.
Type: Damage modifier: Armor
Affects: Self
Frost Storm
Enemies near Shade of Mephisto are Slowed by 40% for 2 seconds. Hitting enemies with Skull Missile while Shade of Mephisto is active increases its Slow to 50%.
Type: CC
Affects: Enemies
Properties: Slow
Radius: 3
Trickery
Shade of Mephisto grants 20% Movement Speed while active and for 2.5 seconds after it ends. After Shade of Mephisto expires, all Slows and Roots are removed from Mephisto.

Consume Souls

Durance of Hate

Durance of Hate spreads outwards from its initial target, Rooting and damaging additional nearby enemy Heroes.
13
Abhorred Skull
Hitting a Hero with Skull Missile grants 20% Spell Power for 6 seconds.
Type: Damage modifier: Spell Power
Affects: Self
Hysteria
Lord of Hatred also reduces the cooldown of Mephisto's Heroic Ability.
Shard of Hate
Basic Attacks hit enemies near the primary target and activate Lord of Hatred, reducing Basic Ability cooldowns by 1 second per Hero hit.
Splash radius: 3.5
16
Lightning Reaction
Skull Missile explodes on contact with Lightning Nova, dealing 138 damage to nearby enemies.
Type: Damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Properties: Area of Effect
Radius: 3
  • Lightning Reaction can trigger multiple times on the same cast.
Static Field
When Lightning Nova's damage bonus reaches 40%, enemy Heroes within its radius take damage equal to 10% of their maximum Health.
Type: Damage
Affects: Enemy Heroes
Properties: Percent damage
Animosity
Cooldown: 15 seconds
Activate to extend the duration of Lightning Nova by 2.5 seconds.
Passive: Basic Attacks while Lightning Nova is active cause an immediate pulse of Lightning Nova damage.
Type: Damage
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: No target
Cast time: Instant
  • Basic Attack-created pulses from Animosity do not benefit from Lightning Nova's innate damage multiplier nor create stacks for it.
  • Basic Attack-created pulses count towards Furious Spark hits.
  • Splash attacks from Shard of Hate do not trigger Animosity.
20
Consumed by Hatred
Consume Souls deals bonus damage equal to 10% of the target's missing Health. If Consume Souls kills a Hero it will cast again 1 additional time.
Type: Damage
Scaling: -
Affects: Enemy Heroes
Properties: Percent damage
  • The bonus damage amount is calculated before the main damage is dealt.
  • Free cast does not require channeling.
Unspeakable Horror
Durance of Hate Silences enemies for 3 seconds after its Root expires.
Type: CC
Affects: Enemy Heroes
Properties: Silence
Mimic
While both Shade of Mephisto and Lightning Nova are active, Lightning Nova also casts from the Shade's location.
  • Shade's Lightning Nova benefits from all talents affecting it except Animosity's passive.
