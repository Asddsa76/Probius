Skills

D
Spawn Egg
Cooldown: 15 seconds
Place an Egg at target location, revealing the nearby area. Upon dying, Murky respawns at the Egg after 8 seconds. Murky only grants 25% of a real Hero's experience upon dying. If Murky's Egg is killed, he is revealed to enemies for 15 seconds, and Spawn Egg is placed on cooldown.
Type: Summon
Targeting: Point target
Cast time: 1.5+0 seconds
Range: 1.0
Respawn Egg
Type: Passive
Scaling: 30 per level (health)
Health: 324
Basic Attack damage: -
Movement Speed: -
Sight radius: 6
  • Minions, Mercenaries and Monsters do not attack the Egg.
  • Enemy Structures can attack the Egg, dealing normal damage to it.
  • Egg remains killed until Murky places a new one.
  • Every time Murky respawns in Hall of Storms (due to dying without Egg) a new Respawn Egg is automatically spawned in Hall of Stroms as well.

R
March of the Murlocs
Cooldown: 110 seconds
After 0.75 seconds, Murky commands a legion of Murlocs to march in a target direction, each one leaping onto the first enemy Hero or Structure they find. Each Murloc deals 125 damage and slows its target by 15% for 5 seconds. Murlocs deal 50% damage to Structures.
Type: Spell damage, Crowd control
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Channeled cast (stationary), Damage over Time, Skillshot, Slow
Cast time: 0.75+0.75 seconds
Radius: 0.5 (collision)
Width: 16
Height: 20
Missile speed: 5
Tickrate: 1.0 per second
Damage per tick: 25
  • Interrupting the ability during its cast puts it on a 10 second cooldown.
  • Summons total of 36 invulnerable murlocs over 6.75 seconds.
  • Murlocs spawn in one of 10 possible locations every 0.1875 seconds.
  • Each murloc sticking to a target applies new instances of DoT and slow that tick separately. New applications do not refresh the duration of pre-existing slow and DoTs.
  • Slow stacks additionally up to 5 times.
R
Octo-Grab

Targeting: Unit target
Properties: Stationary channel, Stun, Unstoppable
Cast time: 0.1+Ch+0 seconds
Range: 6
Missile speed: 22
Tickrate: 1.0 per second
  • The cast is interrupted if the target moves past 2 units of its maximum range.
