Skills

Essence Collection
Cooldown: 5 seconds
Gain 10 Essence from Takedowns and 2 Essence from nearby Minions dying. Activate to consume all Essence and heal yourself for 29 Health over 5 seconds for each Essence stored. Maximum of 50 Essence. Can be cast during Drag and Burrow.
    Type: Healing
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Self
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Heal over Time
    Cast time: Instant
    Tickrate: 2 / second (x11)
Q
Drag
75 Mana Cooldown: 15 seconds
Lash out with your tongue, dealing 160 damage to the first enemy hit, stunning and dragging them with you for 1.75 seconds. If you are Stunned or Silenced while using Drag, the effect ends.
    Type: Damage
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Clamping, Mobile channel, Skillshot
    Channel time (max): 1.75 seconds
    Cast time: 0.1875+Ch+0 seconds
    Range: 6
    Width: 1.25
W
Dark Swarm
50 Mana Cooldown: 10 seconds
Deal 47 damage every 0.5 seconds to nearby enemies for 3.5 seconds. While active, you are able to move through units. Can be cast during Drag and Burrow.
    Type: Damage
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemies
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Area of Effect
    Cast time: Instant
    Range: 3.5
    Tickrate: 2 / second (x6)
E
Burrow
65 Mana Cooldown: 20 / 0.5 seconds
Burrow into the ground, entering Stasis and becoming Invulnerable for 2 seconds.
    Type: Stasis
    Affects: Self
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Stationary channel
    Cast time: Instant
    Reactivate: Cancel Burrow - Cancels Burrow
  • Removes most debuffs from Dehaka on cast.
R
Isolation
70 Mana Cooldown: 60 seconds
Launch biomass that hits the first enemy Hero dealing 200 damage, Silencing and Slowing them 30% for 3 seconds. Only allows them to see a very short distance for 6 seconds.
    Type: Damage
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Enemy Heroes
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Clamping, Skillshot
    Cast time: 0.375+0.1875 seconds
    Range: 8
    Width: 1.75
    Supressed sight radius: 3.5
  • Enemy Heroes hit by Isolation are revealed for 3 seconds.
R
Adaptation
70 Mana Cooldown: 60 seconds
After 4 seconds, Heal for 100% of the damage Dehaka took over this period.
    Type: Healing
    Scaling: -
    Affects: Self
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Delayed
    Cast time: Instant
Z
Brushstalker
Cooldown: 75 seconds
Activate to burrow to a bush on the Battleground.
Passive: Gain 20% movement speed while in a bush and for 2 seconds after leaving.
    Type: Mobility: Teleport
    Targeting: Point target
    Properties: Channeled cast (stationary)
    Channel time: 2 seconds
    Cast time: Ch+1.5 seconds
    Range: Global
  • Interrupting Brushstalker during it's cast puts it on a 10-second cooldown.
  • While travelling, Dehaka is Invulnerable for 1.5 seconds, removing most debuffs.
