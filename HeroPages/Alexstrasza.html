Skills

D
Dragonqueen
Cooldown: 150 seconds
After 1.25 seconds, transform into a dragon and gain 500 Health. While Dragonqueen is active, Alexstrasza's Basic Abilities are empowered, her Basic Attacks deal 143 damage and heal allied Heroes for 43 in an arc in front of her, and the duration of incoming Slows, Roots, and Stuns is reduced by 50%. Lasts 15 seconds.
    Type: Transformation
    Scaling: 4.00%
    Affects: Self
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Attack modifier, CC reduction, Damage, Healing, Splash, Stationary channel (cast)
    Cast time: 1.25+0.5 seconds
    Splash arc: 50°
  • Interrupting Dragonqueen during its cast puts it on a 10-second cooldown.
  • Dragonqueen grants Alexstrasza a different set of Basic Abilities, which have independent cooldowns and are unaffected by Alexstrasza's Talents that enhance her regular Basic Abilities.
  • Dragonqueen's cooldown starts when the ability is successfully cast.
  • Dragonqueen reduces attack speed to 0.80, resulting in increase of single-target Basic Attack DPS by roughly 57%.
  • The moment the ability is successfully cast, and upon transformation back to human form, Alexstrasza enters a special Stasis for 0.5 seconds, removing debuffs and becoming invulnerable and untargetable for this period.

R
Cleansing Flame
100 Mana Cooldown: 100 seconds
After 1.25 seconds, take to the sky and drop fireballs at the position of your mouse cursor every 1.2 seconds for 6 seconds. Each fireball restores 300 Health to nearby allied Heroes, and deals 135 damage to nearby enemies. 2 seconds after dropping all fireballs, Alexstrasza lands at the position of your mouse cursor.
    Type: Damage / Healing
    Scaling: 4.00% (both)
    Affects: Enemies / Allied Heroes
    Targeting: No target
    Properties: Area of Effect, Stasis, Stationary channel (cast)
    Cast time: 1.25+0 (Elf form) / Instant (Dragon form)
    Impact radius: 3
    Retarget range: 20
  • Interrupting Cleansing Flame during its cast period puts it on a 10-second cooldown.
  • Cleansing Flames cooldown starts when the ability is successfully cast.
  • Dragonqueens duration is paused during Cleansing Flame but its cooldowns are not.
