Skills
Minigun
*Cooldown 12 seconds*
Activate to have your Basic Attacks against Heroes deal bonus damage equal to 2.5% of their maximum Health. Lasts 3 seconds.
  • Bonus damage from Minigun is dealt in a separate instance of Spell damage that is flagged to ignore any Armor.
  • Minigun duration starts ath the first Basic Attack after activating the trait. Cooldown starts when the duration ends.

Overkill
60 Mana *Cooldown 12 seconds*
Deal 552 (+4% per level) damage to the target and 276 (+4% per level) damage to nearby targets over 4 seconds. Reactivate to select a new target. Can move and use Abilities while channeling.
  • Deals 46 damage to the primary target and 23 damage to the secondary targets every 0.33 seconds.

Drakken Laser Drill
100 Mana *Cooldown 100 seconds*
Call down a Laser Drill to attack nearby enemies, dealing 142 (+4% per level) damage every second. Reactivate to assign a new target. Lasts 22 seconds.
  • Drakken Laser Drill is a stationary Summon.
  • Damage is dealt every 0.5 seconds in ticks of 71 damage Spell damage.
  • Drakken Laser Drill takes 2 seconds to set up. This makes its effective duration 20 seconds.
  • Drakken Laser Drill has 1255 (+4% per level) Health.
  • Cooldown starts when the ability is cast.
R
Commandeer Odin
100 Mana *Cooldown 100 seconds*
Call down an Odin to pilot. The Odin deals increased Damage, has 100% increased Basic Attack range, and uses different Abilities. The Odin has 25 Armor and lasts 23 seconds.
  • It takes Tychus 2.75 seconds to enter the Odin, during which Tychus is Unstoppable. Armor bonus is instant.
  • While piloting Odin, Tychus' Basic attack damage is set to 191 (+4% per level) and Attack Speed to 1.00/s.
  • Tychus cannot use Minigun and Sizzling Attacks while piloting Odin.
  • Odin's attack speed benefits from Master Assassin talent.
  • Odin's Abilities use no mana.
  • Cooldown starts when the ability is cast.
