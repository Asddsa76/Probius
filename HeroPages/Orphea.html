Skills

D
Overflowing Chaos
Hitting an enemy Hero with a Basic Ability grants 1 Chaos. Chaos can stack up to 3 times. While Orphea has Chaos, her Basic Attacks against Heroes consume all Chaos, dealing 50% increased damage per stack, and healing for 100% of the damage dealt.
Type: Attack modifier, Healing, Damage Modifier: Physical damage
Scaling: -
Affects: Self
Properties: Lifesteal
  • Multiple instances of Chaos stack additively with each other.
  • Stacks multiplicatively with other Physical damage modifiers.
Q
Shadow Waltz
40 Mana Cooldown: 6 seconds
After 0.5 seconds, deal 165 damage to enemies in a line. Hitting a Hero with Shadow Waltz sets its cooldown to 2 seconds, refunds 40 Mana, and causes Orphea to dash a short distance upon moving.
Type: Spell damage / Mobility: Dash
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies / Self
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping
Cast time: 0.5+0 seconds
Area of Effect: Rectangle
Width: 1.25
Height: 8.75
Dash duration: 0.1875 seconds
Dash speed: 20
Dash distance: 3.75
  • Cannot be self-cast.
  • Will drop all commands, except attack commands, which target is still within Orpheas attack range.
  • If the target is a hero, Waltz Dash is enabled for 2 seconds. During this time the first movement command issued, including including ability and attack orders that require her to move in their range, causes Orphea to dash in that direction.
  • Orphea cannot attack while dashing.
W
Chomp
60 Mana Cooldown: 10 seconds
After 0.625 seconds, deal 285 damage to nearby enemies in front of Orphea.
Type: Spell damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping
Cast time: 0.625+0 seconds
Area of Effect: Custom
Inner width: 6*
Outer width: 3*
Height: 5*
  • *)The accurate Area of Effect is done by a Vertex, it's a mirrored area drawn by points (-3,0), (-3,-2), (-2.5,-3.5) (-1.5,-4.5) and (0,-5) in order.
  • Will drop all movement commands, but not attack commands.
E
Dread
50 Mana Cooldown: 14 seconds
Release a wave of dread that deals 85 damage to enemies hit. Dread erupts 0.75 seconds after reaching the end of its path, dealing 175 damage and Slowing enemies in the area by 25% for 2 seconds.
Type: Spell damage, Crowd control
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect, Clamping, Self-castable, Skillshot (piercing), Slow
Cast time: 0.125+0
Range: 12
Area of Effect:
Path - Rectangle
End area - Circle
Radius: 2.5 (end area)
Hitbox: 3.5x1.5 (path)
Missile speed: 17
  • Damage from Dread is applied before the Slow.
  • Enemies can be hit by path only once per cast.

Type: Spell damage

Area of Effect: Circle
Radius: 4.5
  • Drops all movement commands, but not attacks.

Crushing Jaws
75 Mana Cooldown: 70 seconds
After 1.25 seconds, pull enemies in an area towards the center, dealing 250 damage and Stunning them for 0.5 seconds.
Type: Spell damage, Crowd control
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect, Knockback, Stun
Cast time: 0.125+0 seconds
Range: 9
Area of Effect: Rectangle
Pull area width: 10
Damage area width: 4

Dead Magic
Basic Attacks that consume Chaos ignore Armor and deal 115 Spell Damage to all enemies around the target.
Type: Attack modifier / Spell damage
Scaling: - / - / 4.00%
Affects: Self / Enemies
Properties: Armor piercing, Splash
Splash radius: 2.5
  • Armor piercing and splash are two separate effects. Armor piercing only affects the primary target and the splash damage secondary targets.
  • Armor piercing ignores both positive and negative Armor effects.
  • Splash is unaffected by lifesteal from Overflowing Chaos and damage amplification from Ancestral Strength. It can still be affected by other spell damage modifiers, like (Spell) Armor and Spell Power.

Affects: Self
  • Each application refreshes the timer.
