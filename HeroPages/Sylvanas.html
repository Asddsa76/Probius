Skills

Black Arrows
Cooldown: 40 seconds
Activate to cause all Basic Attacks and Abilities to Stun Minions, non-Elite Mercenaries, and Structures for 3 seconds. Lasts for 10 seconds.
Banshee's Curse
Basic Attacks infect enemies with Banshee's Curse for 3 seconds, stacking up to 3 times. Deal 25% more damage to enemies with 3 stacks.
Q
Withering Fire
50 Mana Cooldown: 11 seconds
Shoot the closes enemy up to 5 times over 1.5 seconds for 39 damage, prirotizing Heroes. Cooldown is reset on getting a Takedown.
Type: Spell damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Range: 8
  • Can only be used if an enemy is in 8 range.
  • Consumes charges every .375 seconds.
  • The ability is disabled until you use all charges.
W
Shadow Dagger
50 Mana Cooldown: 11 seconds
Throw a dagger at a target enemy thatl deals 30 damage and an additional 150 damage over 2.5 seconds. Damage dealt by Sylvanas to the initial target spreads Shadow Dagger to all nearby enemies.
Type: Spell damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Cast time: 0.1875
Range: 8
Tickrate: 2 per second
  • The damage is done on impact and a DoT ticks 5 times during 2.5 seconds.
  • On impact, the main target gets to spread the debuff over 2.5 seconds. They are shown with an unique icon on top. The spread is done as a damage response and affects units in a 3.5 radius, extended by the main target unit's radius. It validates if the units are not a wall, invulnerable, cloaked and not revealed, has shadow dagger from the caster Sylvanas. If they fit all that, impact them with the spread one tick later.
E
Haunting Wave
75 Mana Cooldown: 11 seconds
Send forth a wave of banshees dealing 114 damage to all targets. Reactivate to teleport to the banshees' location.
Type: Spell damage, Mobility: Launch
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies, Self
Cast time: 0.1
Range: 14
Tickrate: 2 per second
  • The cast timer for the second activation is 1.75 seconds. If you press E during that time, you'll teleport to the Banshee.
R
Wailing Arrow
100 Mana Cooldown: 90 seconds
Shoot an arrow that can be reactivated to deal 228 damage and silence enemies in an area making them unable to use Abilities for 2.5 seconds. The arrow detonates automatically when it reaches maximum range.
R
Mind Control
75 Mana Cooldown: 50 seconds
After 0.25 seconds, fire a missile that Mind Controls the first enemy Hero hit. Heroes hit are Silenced, Slowed by 30%, and forced to walk towards Sylvanas for 1.75 seconds.

Talents

1
Might of the Banshee Queen
Hitting an enemy with Withering Fire increases Sylvanas's Attack Speed and Spell Power by 5% for 6 seconds, stacking to 5 times.
Unfurling Shadows
❢ Quest: Each time an enemy with 3 stacks of Banshee's Curse is hit by Shadow Dagger, its damage is permanently increases by 0.5%.
Overwhelming Affliction
Enemy Heroes with 3 stacks of Banshee's Curse are Slowed by 15%. Sylvanas's Basic Attacks against Slowed enemy Heroes deal 1% of their maximum Health as damage.
4
Unstable Poison
Minions that die under the effects of Shadow Dagger or Sylvanas's Traits explode, dealing 130 damage to nearby Minions, Mercenaries, and Summons.
Mercenary Queen
Friendly non-Elite Mercenaries near Sylvanas deal 60% more damage. Sylvanas Stuns and deals 30% additional damage to Mercenaries with 3 stacks of Banshee's Curse.
Possession
Charge Cooldown: 12 seconds
Activate to force an enemy Minion to fight for Sylvanas's team. Catapults require 3 charges. Stores up to 3 charges.
Affects: Minion, Catapult
Cast time: 0.1
Range: 10
  • Possessing a minion technically counts as killing it and putting a new summon in its place.
  • Possession awards the XP from the minion kill.
  • The enemy team does not get XP or stacks from killing the possessed minion.
  • Possession fully heals the minion and removes the raven curse on Cursed Hollow.
  • If the unit is the wizard minion, it spawns a Regeneration Globe.
  • An opposing Sylvanas cannot "repossess" the minions you've possessed.
7
Barbed Shot
Hitting the same enemy with 5 shots of Whitering Fire causes the 5th shot to deal 350% bonus damage.
Lost Soul
Basic Attacks against Heroes with 3 stacks of Banshee's Curse reduce the cooldown of Shadow Dagger by 1.75 seconds.
Festering Wounds
Increase Hauting Wave's damage by 25%. Hauting Wave applies 3 stacks of Banshee's Curse.
10
Wailing Arrow
Cooldown: 90 seconds
Shoot an arrow that can be reactivated to deal 228 damage and silence enemies in an area making them unable to use Abilities for 2.5 seconds. The arrow detonates automatically when it reaches maximum range.
Mind Control
Cooldown: 50 seconds
After 0.25 seconds, fire a missile that Mind Controls the first enemy Hero hit. Heroes hit are Silenced, Slowed by 30%, and forced to walk towards Sylvanas for 1.75 seconds.
13
Cold Embrace
Shadow Dagger reduces the Armor of the initial target by 25 for and any enemy it spreads to by 10 for 2.5 seconds.
Windrunner
Hauting Wave can be cast a second time for free within 5 seconds after teleporting. Teleporting with Hauting Wave resets the cooldown of Withering Fire.
Remorseless
Increase Sylvanas's Basic Attack range by 1. Sylvanas's Basic Attacks against enemy Heroes with 3 stacks of Banshee's Curse shoot an untalented shot of Withering Fire. This shot of Withering Fire applies Banshee's Curse.
16
Evasive Fire
Hitting an enemy with Withering Fire grants 6% Movement Speed for 2 seconds, up to 30%. Basic Attacks refresh this duration.
Life Drain
Heal for 15% of damage dealt by Sylvanas to enemies with 3 stacks of Banshee's Curse. Healing is double against Heroes.
Will of the Forsaken
Cooldown: 60 seconds
Activate to become Unstoppable and gain 40% Movement Speed for 3 seconds.
20
Deafening Blast
Enemies at the center of Wailing Arrow's explosion take 50% more damage and are silenced for twice as long.
Dark Lady's Call
Heroes hit by Mind Control have their vision radius greatly reduced for 5 seconds. Mind Control's cooldown is reduced by 30 seconds.
Withering Barrage
Enemy Heroes hit by Withering Fire reduce its cooldown by 0.75 seconds.
Bolt of the Storm
Cooldown: 70 seconds
Activate to teleport to a nearby location.
