Skills

D
Grappling Hook (Trait)
Cooldown: 25 seconds
Qhira fires a Grappling Hook that pulls her to terrain it contacts. If an enemy Hero is hit, they take 35 damage and Qhira launches at them, kicking them for an additional 108 damage.
Can be used while Revolving Sweep is active.
Type: Spell damage / Movement: Launch
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemy Heroes / Self
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Skillshot
Cast time: Instant
Range: 11
Hitbox: 1.0x1.15
Missile speed: 25
Q
Carnage
40 Mana Cooldown: 6 seconds
Unleash your sword in a targeted direction, continuously dealing 30 damage to enemies caught in its path.
Type: Spell damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect, Stationary channel
Cast time: 0.125+Ch+0 seconds
Area of Effect: Rectangle
Width: 1.25
Height: 8.5
Channel time: 0.5625 seconds
  • The area grows to its maximum length in 4 ticks over 0.25 seconds, dealing damage every tick. After additional 0.3125 seconds the sword is pulled back dealing an additional tick of damage to all targets.
W
Blood Rage
35 Mana Cooldown: 8 seconds
Passive: Basic Attack and Ability damage cause enemies to bleed for 44 damage over 4 seconds. Stacks 5 times.
Active: Qhira deals 32 damage and heals for 85 Health per enemy Hero affected. Damage and healing is increased by 50% per each additional stack on that Hero.
Type: Spell damage / Healing
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies / Self
Targeting: No target
Properties: Damage over Time
Cast time: Instant
Range: Global
Tickrate: 1.0 per second (x4)
Damage per tick: 11
  • Active component is disabled, if no enemies are affected by the passive.
  • Subsequent applications of the DoT add to its damage and refresh its duration.
  • Damage from either Blood Rage's active or passive do not add or refresh any stacks.
  • Damage from both bleed and activation can affect non-Heroes, but do not trigger healing.
  • Damage and healing bonuses from the active component stack additively with itself and multiplicatively with other modifiers.
E
Revolving Sweep
70 Mana Cooldown: 16 / 0.25 seconds
Attach your sword to the first enemy Hero hit, dealing 96 damage and Stunning them for 0.25 seconds. Once attached, Qhira avoids Basic Attacks and becomes immune to all effects, rotating around the target for 2.75 seconds, and dealing 105 damage to any enemies between you and the target.
Reactivate to send you to the target's location, knocking them away, dealing 108 damage and Stunning them for 0.75 seconds.
Type: Spell damage Crowd control / Movement: Launch, Status effect
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemy Heroes / Self
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Knockback, Skillshot, Stun
Cast time: 0.125+0.75 seconds
Range: 8
Radius: 6 (swing)
Hitbox:
- Initial: 1.0x1.15
- Swing: 0.75x5.5
Missile speed: 25 (initial)
Knockback duration: 0.1875
Knockback speed: 12
  • If the ability has not been reactivated or interrupted, swinging stops after completing one full circle and Qhira is placed at range distance from the target.
  • Rotation is always clockwise and at set distance from the target.
  • Qhira is unable to attack, move or use her abilities other than Grappling Hook and Revolving Sweep's reactivation.
  • Swing behaviour is instantly removed, dropping Qhira at her current location (as long as it's on pathable surface), if:
    - the target becomes Unstoppable or Invulnerable
    - the target instantly moves more than 10 units
    - the target is Falstad during Flight or Ragnaros during Molten Core
    - the target or Qhira is put in Stasis or Time Stop
    - Qhira latches on to a Hero or terrain with Grappling Hook.
  • While Qhira is swinging, she is Unstoppable and untargetable, nullifies all incoming damage, Evades Basic Attacks and has no collision.
R
Unrelenting Strikes
60 Mana Cooldown: 75 seconds
Deal 44 damage to all nearby enemies every 0.5 seconds for 2.5 seconds as your sword grows outward. Upon expiring, deal 83 damage to all nearby enemy Heroes and Stun them for 0.75 seconds.
Type: Spell damage, Crowd control
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: No target
Properties: Area of Effect, Mobile channel, Stun
Cast time: Instant
Area of Effect: Circle
Radius:
- Initial: 1.5
- Final: 3.5
Tickrate: 2.0/second (x6)
  • Qhira can activate Blood Rage, while channeling Unrelenting Strikes without interrupting it. Qhira cannot use any other abilities or attack during the channel.
  • First 2 ticks strike at the initial radius, after which it grows in 0.5 increments.
  • Final damage is dealt in a separate instance on the last tick in addition to the periodic damage.
R
Final Strike
45 Mana Cooldown: 40 seconds
After 1 second, deal 415 damage to all enemies in a line. This damage is increased by 25% against enemy Heroes below 50% Health.
Type: Spell damage
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Channeled cast, Skillshot
Cast time: 1.0(ch)+0.5 seconds
Range: 20
Hitbox: 3.0x1.5
Missile speed: 36
  • Interrupting the ability during its cast puts it on a 10 second cooldown.
  • Low health damage bonus stacks additively with other damage modifiers affecting Qhira's spell damage output.
