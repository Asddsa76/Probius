Skills

Sadism (Trait)
Passive: Alarak's Ability damage and self-healing are increased by 100% against enemy Heroes.
❢ Repeatable Quest: Takedowns increase Sadism by 3%, up to 30%. Sadism gained from Takedowns is lost on death.
Type: Damage modifier, Bonus healing
Affects: Self
Max total Sadism: 1000%
  • Benefits all flat Spell damage and healing instances and bonuses from Alarak's Basic and Heroic Abilities and Talents.
  • Stacks additively with itself and other damage modifiers affecting Alarak's spell damage output.
  • Stacks multiplicatively with Healing modifiers and additively with other healing bonuses.
Q
Discord Strike
55 Mana Cooldown: 8 seconds
After a 0.5 second delay, enemies in front of Alarak take 175 damage and are silenced for 1.5 seconds.
Type: Spell damage, Crowd control
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Area of Effect, Silence
Cast time: 0.5+0.125 seconds
Area of Effect: Triangle
Width: 5.5*
Height: 6.5
W
Telekinesis
30 Mana Cooldown: 12 seconds
Vector Targeting
Create a force, pushing Alarak and all enemies hit from the targeted point towards the targeted direction. Deals 48 damage to enemies.
Type: Spell damage, Crowd control / Crowd control
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies / Self
Targeting: Vector Targeting
Properties: Knockback, Skillshot (piercing)
Cast time: Instant
Range: 8
Hitbox: 1.5x0.5
Knockback duration: 0.3125 seconds
Knockback speed: 18
  • Alarak does not get Dazed when using Telekinesis on himself.
  • Self-casting sets the starting point at Alarak's location and pushes him (and nearby enemies) to the direction he is facing.
E
Lightning Surge
25 Mana Cooldown: 6 seconds
Deal 62 damage to an enemy and an additional 100% damage to enemies between Alarak and the target. Restore 70 health for each Hero hit.
Type: Spell damage / Healing
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies / Self
Targeting: Unit taget
Properties: Area of Effect
Cast time: Instant
Range: 10
Area of Effect: Rectangle
Width: 1.5
  • The center bonus stacks additively with Sadism and other damage modifiers.
R
Deadly Charge
60 Mana Cooldown: 45 seconds
After channeling, Alarak charges forward dealing 200 damage to all enemies in his path. Distance is increased based on the amount of time channeled, up to 1.5 seconds.
Issuing a Move order while this is channeling will cancel it at no cost. Taking damage will interrupt the channeling.
Type: Spell damage / Movement: Launch
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies / Self
Targeting: No target / Point target
Properties: Skillshot (piercing), Special channel (stationary)
Cast time: 0+Ch+0 seconds
Range:
- Min: 3
- Max: 18.25
Hitbox: 3.0x1.0
Missile speed: 42
  • Interrupting the channel in any way does not put the ability on cooldown.
  • Mana cost is not consumed until the ability has been fully cast, but can't be initiated, if Alarak has insufficient Mana.
  • Range increases by 0.6354 for every 0.0625 seconds channeled.
  • Ability can be channeled indefinitely, but distance will no longer increase after reaching maximum distance at 1.5 second mark.
R
Counter-Strike
50 Mana Cooldown: 30 seconds
Alarak targets an area and channels for 1 second, becoming Protected and Unstoppable. After, if he took damage from an enemy Hero, he sends a shockwave that deals 275 damage.
Type: Spell damage / Status effect
Scaling: 4.00%
Affects: Enemies / Self
Targeting: Point target
Properties: Protected, Stationary channel, Unstoppable
Cast time: Instant
Range: 13
Area of Effect: Triangle
Width: 4.6*
Height: 13
Missile speed: 16
  • Hitscan is done in 3 ticks over 0.1825 seconds, ranging from 0 to 6 on first, 6 to 9 on second and 9 to 13 on third tick.

Chaos Reigns

Counter-Strike
Cooldown: 30 seconds
Alarak targets an area and channels for 1 second, becoming Protected and Unstoppable. After, if he took damage from an enemy Hero, he sends a shockwave that deals 275 damage.

Blade of the Highlord
Basic Attacks against Heroes increase Sadism by 2% up to 40%.
Sadism gained from Blade of the Highlord is lost on death.
Type: Attack modifier (Heroes)
Pure Malice
Increase Sadism by 10% any time an allied Hero dies, up to 40%.
Sadism gained from Pure Malice is lost on death.
Rite of Rak'Shir
Cooldown: 300 seconds
Activate to mark an enemy Hero for 300 seconds.
Hitting the marked Hero with Discord Strike increases Sadism by 3%. Killing the marked Hero increases Sadism by 5% and lowers the cooldown of Rite of Rak'Shir to 10 seconds.
Sadism gained from Rite of Rak'Shir is lost on death.
Type: Active
Affects: Enemy Heroes
Targeting: Unit target
Cast time: Instant
Range: 8
Max stacks:
- Marked hits: 100 (300%)
- Marked Takedowns: 50 (250%)
16
Lethal Onslaught
Hitting an enemy Hero with Discord Strike grants 50% of Sadism's damage bonus to Alarak's Basic Attacks for 4 seconds. Basic Attacks against Heroes refresh this duration.
Type: Attack modifier (enemies), Damage modifier
Affects: Self
Lightning Barrage
Hitting an enemy Hero with the center of Lightning Surge allows it to be cast again for free on a different primary target within the next 2 seconds. This free cast cannot benefit from Lightning Barrage.
  • Second cast benefits from all other talents affecting Lightning Surge.
Mocking Strikes
Basic Attacks against Stunned, Silenced, Slowed, or Rooted Heroes reduce the cooldown of Alarak's Basic Abilities by 3 seconds.
Type: Attack modifier (Heroes)
Affects: Self
20
Counter-Strike
Cooldown: 30 seconds
Alarak targets an area and channels for 1 second, becoming Protected and Unstoppable. After, if he took damage from an enemy Hero, he sends a shockwave that deals 275 damage.
This ability will take over Alarak's Trait button.
Deadly Charge
Cooldown: 45 seconds
After channeling, Alarak charges forward dealing 200 damage to all enemies in his path. Distance is increased based on the amount of time channeled, up to 1.5 seconds.
Issuing a Move order while this is channeling will cancel it at no cost. Taking damage will interrupt the channeling.
This ability will take over Alarak's Trait button.
Last Laugh
Cooldown: 30 seconds
Active: Activate teleport to the chosen location and remove all Roots, Slows, and damage over time effects. If Alarak fails to hit enemy Heroes 3 times with Basic Abilities within 4 seconds of using Last Laugh, his Health is reduced to 1.
Type: Movement: Teleport
Affects: Self
Targeting: Point target
Properties: CC removal
Cast time: Instant
Range: 12
  • Hit requirement is the sum of all Heroic targets hit, with any of his Basic Ablities, within the time limit.
  • Health reduction ignores all damage reduction and prevention effects.
Hasty Bargain
Cooldown: 20 seconds
Active: Activate to permanently reduce Alarak's Sadism by 4% and reset the cooldowns of his Basic Abilities.
Type: Active
Targeting: No target
Cast time: Instant
  • Hasty Bargain cannot be used, if Alarak doesn't have enough Sadism.
  • Alarak's innate Sadism is consumed first. If there is not enough innate Sadism, Hasty Bargain can consume Sadism gained from Takedowns and Talents.
