D
Misha, Focus!
Cooldown: 0.25 seconds
Passive:Misha gains 15% move speed.
Command Misha to attack a specific enemy or move to a point and wait.
Targeting Rexxar commands Misha to retreat to his position, gaining 30% move speed until she reaches Rexxar.
Targeting Misha commands her to hold her current position.
    Type: -
    Targeting: Point target / Unit target
    Properties: Clamping
    Cast time: Instant
    Radius: Global

Misha
    Type: Summon
    Properties: Heroic Pet
    Statistics: Data:Misha
    Sight radius: 7
    Leash radius: 17.25
  • Killing Misha rewards a Takedown credit and 0.25 Hero kills/XP.
  • Killing Rexxar rewards a Takedown credit and 0.75 Hero kills/XP, when Misha is dead. If Misha is alive while Rexxar is killed, she dies as well, totaling a full Hero kill/XP.
  • Rexxar becomes is unable to control Misha, while he is unable to act (Stunned, Dazed or put in Time Stop or Stasis, other than Feign Death) or Silenced.
  • If Rexxar enters a vehicle or is some other way removed from the battleground, Misha will be removed from the Battleground as well.
  • Issuing Move and Attack commands to Misha doesn't break channels, using abilities will.
  • Rexxar and Misha share the effects of Healing Fountains and Regeneration Globes.
