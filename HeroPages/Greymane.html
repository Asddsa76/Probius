Skills
Curse of the Worgen (Trait)
Use certain Abilities to shapeshift between a Human and a Worgen. While Human, your Basic Attack is ranged. While Worgen, your Basic Attack is melee but deals 40% more damage and you gain 10 Armor, taking 10% less damage.
    Worgen Basic Attack Range: 1.2
  • Successfully mounting in the Worgen form shapeshifts you into Human.
  • Any shapeshift places Darkflight and Disengage on full cooldown.
Q
Razor Swipe
25 Mana *Cooldown 4 seconds*
Swipe towards the area in front of you for 126 (+4% per level) damage. Human Form: Gilnean Cocktail
  • Usable only in Worgen form.
  • Razor Swipe can move through units.
Q
Gilnean Cocktail
55 Mana *Cooldown 9 seconds*
Hurl a flask that deals 55 (+4% per level) damage to the first enemy hit and explodes for 220 (+4% per level) damage to enemies in a cone behind them. Worgen Form: Razor Swipe
  • Usable only in Human form.
W
Inner Beast
70 Mana *Cooldown 20 seconds*
Gain 50% Attack Speed for 3 seconds. Basic Attacks refresh this duration, and reduce the cooldown of Inner Beast by 0.5 seconds.
E
Disengage
*Cooldown 5 seconds*
Roll away and shapeshift into a Human. Human Form: Darkflight
  • Usable only in Worgen form.
  • Shares cooldown with Darkflight.
E
Darkflight
10 Mana *Cooldown 5 seconds*
Shapeshift into a Worgen and leap at an enemy dealing 88 (+4% per level) damage. Worgen Form: Disengage
  • Usable only in Human form.
  • Shares cooldown with Disengage.
R
Go for the Throat
90 Mana *Cooldown 80 seconds*
Leap at an enemy Hero and shapeshift into a Worgen, slashing for 355 (+4% per level) total damage. If this kills them, the Ability can be used a second time within 10 seconds for free.
  • Enemy heroes with enough low HP to be killed by using this ability are indicated by a red skull above them if the ability is not on cooldown.
  • Greymane is Unstoppable for the leap duration
R
Cursed Bullet
50 Mana *Cooldown 30 seconds*
Greymane shapeshifts into a Human and fires a bullet that hits the first enemy Hero in its path, dealing 40% of their current Health in damage. Does not affect Vehicles.
